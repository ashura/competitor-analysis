## Basic Information

Sparkonix (India) Pvt. Ltd. is a pioneer in the development and manufacturing of Electric Discharge Machines (EDMs), specializing in both Micro-controller and ZNC EDMs, CNC EDM Drills, and EDM Tap Removal Machines. Established in the 1960s, the company introduced indigenous Metal Arc Disintegrators to India and now offers a wide range of industry-leading EDM products for various mould making and machining applications. With more than 7,500 installations across the globe and dealers in major Indian cities like Delhi, Kolkata, and Chennai, Sparkonix serves over 30 industries and supports 500+ applications. The company is headquartered at B-4, H Block, MIDC, Pimpri, Pune - 18, India.
