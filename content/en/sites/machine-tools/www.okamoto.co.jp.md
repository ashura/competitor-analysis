## Basic Information

Established in 1935, Okamoto Machine Tool Works, Ltd. (株式会社 岡本工作機械製作所) is headquartered in Annaka, Gunma Prefecture, Japan. Originally founded as a gear grinding machine manufacturer, the company has developed into a total abrasive processing machine manufacturer, producing a wide range of machine tools and semiconductor processing equipment. Okamoto is renowned for supporting high-quality, high-precision machining worldwide, blending long-standing expertise with the latest advancements in manufacturing technology. The company operates an integrated manufacturing system, handling processes from wooden pattern design and casting to machining, painting, and final assembly in-house. Okamoto has a global presence, serving a diverse range of industries, and is often referred to as a "mother machine" manufacturer due to its foundational role in enabling new manufacturing epochs.

## Key Features

Okamoto Machine Tool Works, Ltd. specializes in the design, manufacturing, and global supply of high-precision grinding machines and semiconductor processing equipment. Key features and main capabilities include:

- **Integrated manufacturing system:** The company controls all stages from casting and component machining to painting and final assembly, ensuring high quality and reliability ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/production/)).
- **Precision and innovation:** Okamoto leverages both longstanding expertise and the latest machining technology to deliver reliable, high-quality products used worldwide ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/)).
- **Diverse product lineup:** Their grinding machines serve various industries, supporting a wide range of manufacturing applications ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/products/)).
- **Structural excellence:** Machines feature a T-shaped monocoque structure for rigidity and accuracy, utilizing ball screw drives and linear roller guides for precise positioning ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/products/psg-ca3/)).
- **Advanced grinding solutions:** Standard machine capabilities include flat automatic cycle grinding, shift plunge grinding, traverse grinding, multi-position grinding, and more ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/products/psg-ca3/)).
- **Global reach:** Over 60,000 machines are in operation worldwide, demonstrating the company’s influence and reliability in final-stage precision grinding ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/search-by-machines/)).
- **Semiconductor equipment:** Okamoto is also a significant supplier of machines for semiconductor processing, contributing to high-precision manufacturing in the tech sector ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/company/)).

Sources: All information is cited directly from the official Okamoto Machine Tool Works website ([https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/)).

## Technical Strength

Okamoto Machine Tool Works, Ltd. demonstrates technical strength through several key aspects:

1. **In-house design and production:** Wooden patterns for machine casting bases are designed and produced internally, allowing for highly rigid grinding machines ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/production/)).
2. **Experience:** Over 90 years in the field, evolving from a gear grinding machine company to a comprehensive "total abrasive processing machine manufacturer" ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/company/)).
3. **Global recognition:** More than 60,000 machines operating worldwide reflects their industry leadership ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/search-by-machines/)).
4. **Technical innovations:** Use of angular contact bearings in grinding wheel spindles for greater rigidity and minimal deflection, ensuring stable performance even with hard-to-cut materials ([source](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/products/ogm-ncag3/)).
