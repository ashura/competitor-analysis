## Basic Information

Founded in 1989, TOP-ONE Machinery Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer of CNC milling machines based in Taichung City, Taiwan (No. 2-161, Shenqing Rd., Qingshui Dist., Taichung City 43641, Taiwan). The company employs over 100 people in Taiwan and Mainland China, with branch offices and service teams globally. They have expanded with factories in Kunming (since 1995), Shanghai (since 2001), and service points throughout China. Their product range covers CNC vertical milling machines, bed mills, vertical alloy wheel lathes, ID/OD grinding machines, and column machines, catering to industries such as aerospace, automotive, medical, energy, shipbuilding, and more. Their commitment to quality, customer service, and technical innovation has established them as a trusted partner in the global machinery and mold processing sectors.
