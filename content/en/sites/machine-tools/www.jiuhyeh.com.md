## Basic Information

Jiuh-Yeh Precision Machinery Co., Ltd. (鉅業精機股份有限公司), located in Taichung City, Taiwan, is a reputable supplier and manufacturer of milling machines and machining centers. Known under the brand name Millstar, the company offers a wide range of products including vertical and horizontal machining centers, bed type milling machines, new and conventional knee type milling machines, and various CNC and conventional models. With a focus on innovation, precision, and customized service, Jiuh-Yeh serves global markets in Asia, the Americas, and Europe. They emphasize quality assurance and hold ISO certification. The company values permanent service, quick support, and global reach in its business philosophy.

## Key Features

Jiuh-Yeh Precision Machinery Co., Ltd. specializes in the manufacturing of various milling machines and machining centers, including vertical machining centers (BMV/BV and LMV/LV series), horizontal machining centers (JMH, LMH, BMH series), bed type milling machines (VH, VT, V series), new knee type and conventional knee type milling machines, as well as PC/CNC base milling machines. Key features of Jiuh-Yeh’s products include: robust designs with finite element analysis, high-strength cast iron columns, precision-ground tables, hardened slide surfaces, integrated automatic lubricating systems, cooling systems, high speed and precision ball screws, AC servo-driven axes, and use of roller type linear guideways for high rigidity and precision. The company emphasizes innovation, customization, modular technology, and lifelong/permanent customer service. Their products are renowned for stability, safety (limit switches), and high durability, serving global markets in Asia, the Americas, and Europe.
(Source: https://www.jiuhyeh.com)

## Featured Products

Based on the official website of Jiuh-Yeh Precision Machinery Co., Ltd. ([https://www.jiuhyeh.com/](https://www.jiuhyeh.com/)), the company's feature products include:

1. **Vertical Machining Centers (BMV/BV series, LMV/LV series):**
   - Advanced machining centers with stable base and box way support for high precision.
2. **Horizontal Machining Centers (JMH, LMH, BMH series):**
   - Designs focused on vibration reduction, precision, and high rigidity, with CNC rotary table options.
3. **Bed Type Milling Machines (VH, VT, V series):**
   - Machines with integrated automatic lubrication, cooling systems, hardened slide surfaces, and rigid construction for stability.
4. **Knee Type Milling Machines (2VHT, and new types):**
   - Manufactured with high quality, tempered cast iron. Equipped with precision ground tables and high-stability spindles. AC servo motor-driven X and Y axes for smooth traverse.
5. **PC/CNC Base Conventional Milling Machines (VH PC/CNC, VT PC/CNC, V PC/CNC series)**
6. **Customized Machining Centers:**
   - Solutions tailored to customer requirements, utilizing modular design for productivity and precision.

**Detailed product examples:**
- **VT550 Bed Type Milling Machine:** Features automatic lubricating system, cooling system, axis limit switches, and hardened slides for stable performance.
- **2VHT Knee Type Milling Machine:** High-quality cast iron construction, precision ground table, SCM-21 alloy spindle, and AC servo motor-driven axes.
- **BMV1000 Vertical Machining Center:** Utilizes finite element analysis, has a stable base, multiple box guideway blocks, and high precision ball screws.
- **JMH800 Horizontal Machining Center:** Moving column structure, direct spindle, roller type linear guideways, CNC rotary table for efficient workpiece change.
