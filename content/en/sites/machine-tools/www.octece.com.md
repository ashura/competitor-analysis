## Basic Information

OCEAN TECHNOLOGIES CO., LTD. is a Taiwan-based company specializing in the design and manufacture of CNC electric discharge machines (EDMs). The company offers a broad range of EDM products such as CNC fast hole drilling machines, micro hole EDM machines, and super drill EDM machines, catering to industries including aerospace, wind power, and precision microscopy. Their headquarters is located at No. 1-1, Zhong Zheng Rd., Wu-Feng Dist., Taichung City, Taiwan 41361. Ocean Technologies emphasizes technological innovation and high-quality manufacturing to meet diverse industrial needs.

## Key Features

OCEAN TECHNOLOGIES CO., LTD. specializes in the manufacturing of CNC electric discharge machines (EDMs), offering a broad range of solutions tailored for industrial applications. The company's product lines, such as the River 1000, River 35, River 800, River 600, and River 350, are designed to meet diverse drilling and machining requirements. ## Environment / Society / Governance

OCEAN TECHNOLOGIES CO., LTD. demonstrates a comprehensive commitment to ESG (environmental, social, and governance) principles according to its 2017 Corporate Social Responsibility Report:

**Environment:**
- Achieved ISO 14001 environmental management certification and conducts annual environmental assessments focused on energy-saving and pollution prevention.
- Developed products such as the HP1 Sparking Circuit, leading to a 40% increase in processing speed and 30% less energy consumption, supporting green product leadership and energy conservation.
- Introduced innovative production features (e.g., use of insulation paint, waste reduction, and LED lighting upgrades) to minimize environmental impact.
- Waste recycling and resource reuse are standard, hazardous waste is minimized, and suppliers are required to meet environmental protection requirements.
- Tree planting and support of organic farming initiatives are among OCEAN’s broader sustainability activities.

**Society:**
- Invests significantly in employee development: regular training (average 16.94-24.19 hours annually), diverse workplace policies, safety/health programs, and comprehensive benefits (insurance, bonuses, welfare activities, parental leave without pay, retirement plans).
- Fosters a zero-discrimination, anti-bullying culture and provides multiple channels for employee complaints, with no reported violations in human rights or workplace discrimination in 2017.
- Social care is expressed through scholarships, child support, charity events, donations to minority groups, free internships, and support of community activities.

**Governance:**
- Operates a formal corporate governance structure featuring a board of directors, clear risk management, ethics policies, and regular internal audits.
- Supplier Management system requires local procurement (over 90% in Taiwan), supplier audits, and compliance with labor, safety, and environmental standards.
- No violations or fines for legal, environmental, or labor non-compliance in 2017.
- Stakeholder engagement and transparent communication are priorities, with regular stakeholder surveys and consultation.
- Major governance topics include integrity management, anti-corruption, risk management, innovation, health and safety, and responsible procurement.

**Achievements and Recognition:**
- Recipient of multiple industry, research, and innovation awards.
- International sales in 35+ countries, with a major footprint in aerospace and advanced manufacturing.
