## Basic Information

TONG YI MACHINERY INC. (銅翌機械有限公司), also recognized under the Dowell brand, is located at No.263, Jiujia 2nd Rd., Houli Dist, Taichung City 421004, Taiwan (R.O.C). The company specializes in the design and manufacture of surface grinding machines, including manual, semi-auto, automatic, NC/CNC high-precision, and rotary surface grinders, as well as double column grinding machines. Their products serve industries such as aerospace, machinery, automotive, mold, and semiconductors. Emphasizing research and development, quality control, and ongoing innovation, TONG YI MACHINERY INC. collaborates closely with clients to deliver customized solutions and maintain technological leadership.
