## Basic Information

Atrump Machinery Inc. was incorporated in 1989 and is based in Pomona, California, United States. The company specializes in producing CNC and manual machine tools, including bed mills, knee mills, machining centers, precision lathes, universal and MONASET tool and cutter grinders, as well as CNC accessories and controllers. Atrump serves various clients in the industrial manufacturing and machining sectors, providing solutions suited for diverse production environments.

## Technical Strength

Atrump demonstrates significant technical strength through its extensive and diverse product range, serving both traditional and CNC machine tool sectors. Highlights include:

- CNC Bed Mills, Knee Mills, Machining Centers (vertical, double column, horizontal, office machines)
- Wheel Lathes (horizontal and vertical)
- Precision Manual and CNC Lathes
- Tool & Cutter Grinders

Many models, such as the AL, AM, AV, BM, and EL series, are equipped with advanced Centroid controls featuring 15-inch touch screen LCDs, easy conversational programming, and high user interface standards. Machines feature large spindle motors (up to 15 HP), multi-speed gearheads, and quality measures compliant with ISO 9002 and CE standards. Some models feature laser compensation for precision and ATC (Automatic Tool Changer) options for higher efficiency. The equipment is designed for strength, accuracy, and reliability, catering to industries like alloy wheel refurbishment and industrial machining.
