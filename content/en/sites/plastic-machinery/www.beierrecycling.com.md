## Basic Information

BEIER MACHINERY, founded in 1998 and headquartered in Zhangjiagang, Jiangsu province, China, is a major manufacturer specializing in plastic recycling and extrusion equipment. The company operates four main business sections: plastic recycling and washing lines, compounding and conveying systems, blow molding machines, and high-efficiency, energy-saving European-standard extrusion lines. With more than 350 professional employees and a facility covering over 160,000 square meters, BEIER MACHINERY has established itself as a top brand in China and exports products to over 150 countries. The company is recognized for innovation and technology development, having received numerous national and provincial awards in technology, export, and enterprise credibility. It continues to pursue advancements in Industry 4.0, intelligence manufacturing, and global R&D with platforms such as an enterprise R&D center, an engineer research center for plastic recycling, and collaborations involving postdoctoral research workstations. BEIER MACHINERY emphasizes quality, credibility, and win-win cooperation as its core principles.
*Source: [BEIER MACHINERY About Us](https://www.beierrecycling.com/about-us/)*

## Marketing Focus

BEIER MACHINERY focuses on providing comprehensive plastic recycling solutions, including recycling and washing lines for waste plastic materials such as PET bottles, PE films, PP woven bags, ABS, hard plastics, and waste from electronic equipment. They also offer advanced plastic pelletizing and granulating machines for converting recycled plastics into reusable granules for applications like blow molding, injection molding, and extrusion.
