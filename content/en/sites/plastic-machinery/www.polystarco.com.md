## Basic Information

POLYSTAR Machinery Co., Ltd., established in 1989 and headquartered in Tainan City, Taiwan, specializes in designing and manufacturing plastic recycling machines and blown film extrusion equipment. The company offers a range of machinery for recycling various plastics and producing high-quality plastic films. POLYSTAR serves customers globally across packaging, environmental, agricultural, and industrial sectors, positioning itself as an easy-operating, export-oriented manufacturer. Their products are currently in operation in more than 70 countries.

## Key Features

- **Plastic recycling machines:** Efficiently process PE, PP films, raffia, woven, regrind, and hard plastics. Models include single and two-stage recycling machines, shredder-integrated types, and squeezer drying solutions for washed film. Designed for both producers and recyclers for ease of use and high output.
- **Blown film extruder lines:** Capable of producing HDPE, LDPE, and LLDPE films, as well as multi-layered films (ABA, AB) and blending with CaCO₃ and recycled materials. Includes standard, mini, twin head, two-layer AB, and three-layer ABA machines for flexible and high-quality film production.
- **Bag making machines:** Specialized for T-shirt bags, perforated bags, and bottom seal types, offering efficient, automated bag conversion processes.
- **Integrated Solutions:** The company customizes solutions for various plastic recycling materials and film applications, providing technology for both circular economy and sustainable production.
- **Global Support:** Comprehensive customer service, installation, after-sales engineering, and an extensive network of case studies and successful stories across continents.
