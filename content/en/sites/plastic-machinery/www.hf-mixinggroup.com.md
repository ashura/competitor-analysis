## Basic Information

HF GROUP is a leading manufacturer and solutions provider in rubber and plastics processing, headquartered in Germany with 15 sites worldwide and approximately 2,000 employees. The company specializes in high-performance mixing, curing, extrusion, and automation equipment for the tire, rubber, and plastic industries. HF GROUP's core products include BANBURY® tangential mixers, INTERMIX® intermeshing mixers, TANDEM and UMIX® universal mixers, extruders (CONVEX™ twin-screw and SINDEX® single screw), tyre curing presses, cutting machines, and advanced digital solutions like HF Xplore® and MyMixingRoom®. They emphasize sustainability, offering technologies that can reduce CO₂ emissions by up to 60%. HF GROUP regularly participates in leading international expos and has received supplier awards from global companies such as Continental and Michelin.

## Key Features

HF GROUP (HF Mixing Group) is a global leader in high-performance rubber mixing machines and complete mixing room solutions, primarily serving the tire and technical rubber goods industry. The company has a strong engineering heritage spanning over 150 years and is credited with significant innovations such as the Banbury Mixer, intermeshing rotor systems with fixed and variable centers (VIC™), and tandem technology for optimized mixing processes. HF GROUP provides comprehensive and sustainable mixing solutions, including tandem mixing technology, which transfers material from an upper to a lower mixer to optimize capacity and efficiency. The company continues to expand its capabilities in mixing room automation and integrated raw material handling. Headquarters are in Freudenberg, Germany, and it operates as part of the Possehl Group.

## Featured Products

The main feature products of HF GROUP include:

1. **Batch Mixers:** Customized solutions for precise, homogeneous rubber compounds, including BANBURY® tangential mixers, INTERMIX® intermeshing mixers, TANDEM mixers, TECNOLAB® laboratory mixers, and UMIX® universal mixers.
2. **Continuous Mixers:** High-performance plastics and rubber processing equipment utilizing FARREL POMINI® continuous mixing technology.
3. **Tyre Curing Presses:** Innovative modular tyre curing presses such as eCuring Electrical Curing Presses, HF CureMaster® PCR & TBR, HF Classic MCP Modular Curing Presses, Hydraulic Curing Presses, and Post Curing Inflator (PCI). Over 6,000 presses delivered worldwide.
4. **Extruders:** Twin-screw (CONVEX™), single-screw (SINDEX®), and strainer extruders (FILTEX®) designed for reliability and maximum homogeneity in rubber processing.
5. **Cutting Machines:** BALECUT® bale cutter machines and STRIPCUT® strip cutting machines for precise, efficient rubber cutting.
6. **Mixing Mills:** MILLMIX® mixing mills and TECNOLAB® laboratory mixing mills suitable for sheeting and laboratory use.
7. **Controls & Automation:** Solutions such as ADVISE® for mixing room automation and MixVision for rubber compounding control.
8. **Maintenance & Safety:** Maintenance solutions such as mBox® and mChute for enhanced plant safety.

These products are designed primarily for the rubber and tire manufacturing industry, focusing on precision, efficiency, and reliability.
