## Basic Information

Tai Dragon Machinery Co., Ltd. was founded in 1977 and is based in Taichung, Taiwan. The company focuses on the design and manufacture of high-speed horizontal wrapping and packaging machines. Tai Dragon is renowned for pursuing high quality and efficiency, and its machinery meets international standards. The business has developed over 30 models of packaging machines and is known for serving food packaging needs but is also active in expanding to other industries through exhibitions and internet marketing. Tai Dragon has a strong export orientation with a reputation for innovative design and reliable performance.

## Featured Products

Tai Dragon Machinery Co., Ltd. specializes in a broad range of packaging machinery designed for both food and non-food industries. Their featured products include:

1. **Horizontal Flow Wrapper**: Automated packaging machine suited for bakery, biscuits, confectionery, and many other food products. It is designed for efficiency, ease of operation, and is easy to clean.
2. **Box Motion Flow Wrapper**: Provides a stable and efficient solution, suitable for larger or irregularly shaped products, requiring compact and secure packaging.
3. **Top Seal Packaging Machine**: Ideal for applications like medical test kit packaging, where a hermetic seal is crucial for product safety and hygiene.
4. **Automatic Packing Line**: For higher-volume packaging needs, offering flexibility for diverse applications and easy format change.
5. **Continuous Bag Sealing Machine**: Used for continuous, reliable sealing of pre-formed bags.

Applications cover a wide range, including cookie/candy packaging, cake packaging, bakery products, noodles, frozen foods, dumplings, vegetables, napkins, cleaning products, and cutlery. Tai Dragon also offers specialty machines, such as the newly released Desktop Horizontal Packaging Machine, tailored for small-scale operations and ideal for storefronts or workshops.

For more information and detailed descriptions of each product, refer directly to their [official website](https://www.taidragon.com/en/product-categories/PackingMachinery).

## Technical Strength

Tai Dragon Machinery Co., Ltd. demonstrates significant technical strength and competitiveness in automatic packaging machinery. Established in 1989, the company specializes in the research, design, manufacture, and marketing of over 30 types of automated packaging machines, including horizontal flow wrappers, box-motion packaging machines, and top-seal machines. Tai Dragon has an independent R&D and engineering department, enabling them to provide customized and flexible packaging solutions for both food (e.g., cookies, bread, noodles) and non-food (e.g., industrial parts, masks) applications. Their technical focus includes ongoing innovation in software systems for machine stability, high-speed packaging, quick maintenance, and expandability. In recent years, they've also integrated robotic arm systems to enhance automation and production efficiency, reducing labor costs. The company serves global markets through domestic managers and international agents and has pursued advanced technological levels in its production systems, continuously upgrading its technology in response to changing industry needs. Core values include advanced innovation, long-term vision, and continuous technical improvement.
