## Basic Information
Yi Tzung Precision Machinery Corp, originally continuing Kim Ho Sheng Iron Factory founded in 1946, formally changed its name to Yi Tzung in 1979. The company is based at No.601, Chung Shan Rd., Sec.3, Tantzu, Taichung, Taiwan. It is renowned for its professional manufacturing of rubber and plastics machinery, with mechanical processing capabilities such as mixing, kneading, calendaring, coating, sheeting, and cooling. Yi Tzung’s products are widely used in industries producing automotive/motorcycle tires, shoe soles, radiating elements, silicon rubber products, and medical/industrial goods. The company operates its own advanced casting factory, producing about 85% of its components in-house, and implements strict quality control throughout its operations. It is also associated with Eucast, its captive foundry certified with ISO 9001:2000.

## Key Features
Yi Tzung Precision Machinery Corp specializes in the professional manufacturing of rubber and plastics machinery with a long-standing history since 1946. Their key capabilities include major mechanical processing such as mixing, kneading, calendaring, coating, sheeting, and cooling of rubber/EVA materials. Their products are widely applied in industries including automotive (auto and motorcycle tires), footwear (shoe soles), electronic components (quartz screen radiating elements), silicon rubber items, medical, daily use, and general industrial rubber products. Yi Tzung offers a comprehensive machinery range such as High Speed Intensive Mixers, Dispersion Kneaders, Rubber Mixing Mills, Rubber Calendering Machines, Twin Screw Sheeters, Rubber Strainer Machines, Batch Off Machines, Laboratory Dispersion Kneaders, Reclaimed Rubber Machinery, Plastic/Rubber Stock Blenders, Bucket Conveyors, and High Performance Raw Rubber Cutters. The company maintains strict quality control from design and casting to assembly and electrical setup, with over 85% of components self-produced in-house. Yi Tzung also has its own advanced casting factory and is associated with EUCAST foundry (ISO 9001:2000 certified), specializing in functional castings for their machinery.
