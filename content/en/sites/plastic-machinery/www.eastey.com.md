## Basic Information

Eastey, headquartered in Minnesota, United States, is a leading manufacturer of heavy-duty packaging machinery. Their product portfolio includes case tapers, L-bar sealers, sleeve wrappers, shrink tunnels, and bundling equipment, widely used in the food and beverage, electronics, medical, pharmaceutical, and other industries. Eastey is recognized for producing reliable, industrial-grade machines designed for efficiency and durability. The company is part of Engage Technologies, and it has operated for several decades, focusing on packaging solutions both in the US and internationally.

## Key Features

Eastey is a leading manufacturer and supplier of heavy-duty shrink packaging equipment and case tapers. Their product line includes L-sealers, sleeve wrappers, shrink tunnels, bundling equipment, and automatic L-sealers. Key features and main capabilities include:

1. Heavy-duty steel construction for durability
2. Ability to handle various shrink films such as PVC, polyolefin, and polyethylene
3. Versatility with manual, automatic, and angled equipment configurations
4. Variable air flow and air velocity control in shrink tunnels
5. Electronic tension control and pneumatic air cylinders for consistent sealing
6. Equipment designed for industries such as food & beverage, electronics, and pharmaceuticals
7. Easy operation, minimal training or maintenance required, and robust safety features
8. ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system

Eastey is noted for reliable, cost-effective packaging machines widely used in industrial packaging environments.
