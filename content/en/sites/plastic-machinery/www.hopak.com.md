## Basic Information

Founded in 1992 and headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan, HOPAK MACHINERY CO., LTD. is a leading manufacturer specializing in the design, research and development, production, and sales of high-speed horizontal flow wrappers (HFFS) and automated packaging lines. The company provides a wide range of packaging equipment and systems, including digital and compact flow wrappers, serving various industries such as food, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, hardware, publishers, and household products. Known for their durable machines, innovative solutions, and strong after-sales support, HOPAK has earned CE and ISO certifications and established a global presence, exporting their products to over 100 countries.
