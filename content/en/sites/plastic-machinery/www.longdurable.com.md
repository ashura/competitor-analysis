## Basic Information
Founded in 1995 and located in Yunlin County, Taiwan, LONG DURABLE MACHINERY CO., LTD. specializes in the design, manufacturing, and supply of fully automatic packaging machines, including L-type sealers, sleeve-type sealers, shrink tunnels, carton inserters, carton erectors, and packaging equipment for various products. The company has a global presence, exporting to markets such as Japan, Korea, Australia, England, America, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia. With decades of industry experience, it is recognized for its commitment to high standards and innovation in the packaging machinery sector.

## Featured Products
LONG DURABLE MACHINERY CO., LTD. offers a comprehensive range of packaging machinery. Key product categories and notable models include:

1. **Automatic L Sealer Machine**:
   - LA-410F, LA-460+LC-1200, LA-460E+LC-1200E, LA-1000L+LC-2200, LA-700, LA-500A+LC-1000
   - Includes fully automatic L-sealers, side-sealers, vertical type L-sealers, and options for acrylic and extra-long packaging.
2. **Sleeve Type Sealer**:
   - LB-600A+LC-1500, LB-600S+LC-1500, LB-900T+LC-1500, LB-2400, LB-2000F+LC-2000(PE), LB-620+LC-1000B, LB-2000A
   - Semi-/fully-automatic group packaging sealers, PE shrink packaging, and empty bottle packers.
3. **Shrink Tunnel Wrapping Machine**:
   - LC-1000, LC-1000E, LC-1200, LC-1500 (PE), LC-1800 (PE), LC-2000 (PE), LC-3000 (PE)
   - Various sizes and shrink wrapping applications.
4. **Carton Bag Inserting & Sealer Machine**:
   - LE-887, LE-886A, LE-886, LE-889
   - Automatic carton bag inserting and sealing, direct/contrary insert types, carton folding.
5. **Automatic Plastic Packing Machine**:
   - LV-56+LF-720, LF-725, LF-721, LF-726
   - Cup packaging sealers with counters, rectangle product sealers, straw packaging sealers, semi-automatic cup lid packaging.
6. **Special Type Packing Machine**:
   - LG-760, LG-780, LF-720V
   - For paper bowls, plates, mushroom & silver vegetable packaging, horizontal box erectors.
7. **Automatic Palletizers**:
   - LK-900 for different palletizing needs.
8. **Automated Packaging Lines**:
   - Custom lines for CO₂, noodles, cups, sesame oil, and jelly packing.
