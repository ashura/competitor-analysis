## Basic Information

Hangzhou Fuyang Sunrise Machinery Co.,Ltd. (杭州富陽曙光機械有限公司) is a rising star in the field of industrial automation, focusing primarily on manufacturing high-speed straw making and packing machines. Their main product lines include PLA (polylactic acid) straw making machines, biodegradable straw machines, PP straw making machines, as well as straw packing and bending machines. Additional offerings include machines for paper products, cutlery packing, related spare parts, and biodegradable raw materials. The company emphasizes technical innovation and top-quality service, supported by a dedicated R&D and aftersales team to ensure customer satisfaction.

Hangzhou Fuyang Sunrise Machinery operates its own manufacturing facility, Hangzhou Longer Machinery Co.,Ltd. (Brand: NEWTECH), and is located at W-15, No.1707, Wenju Street, Fuyang, Hangzhou, China. Customer support includes pre-sale, in-sale, and after-sale services, with international communication and support channels.
