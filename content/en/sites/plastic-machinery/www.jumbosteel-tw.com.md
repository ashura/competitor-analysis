## Basic Information

JUMBO STEEL MACHINERY CO., LTD. (壯鋼機械股份有限公司) is a Taiwan-based manufacturer that focuses on the design and production of straw making machines, including PLA/biodegradable, PHA/marine degradable, and PP straw machines. Their product line also features straw bending machines, straw packing machines, cotton bud stick machines, lollipop stick making machines, pen refill stick machines, strapping band machines, and PVC heat shrinkable film making machines. With many years of experience and accumulated expertise, the company delivers superior quality and innovative equipment, supporting customers with turnkey solutions for various manufacturing requirements.
