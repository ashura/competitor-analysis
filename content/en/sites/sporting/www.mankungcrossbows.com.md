## Basic Information

Man Kung Enterprise Co., Ltd. is headquartered at 369, Xuetian Rd., Taichung City, Taichung, 414010, Taiwan. The company operates primarily in the foundries and sporting goods manufacturing sector, focusing on archery and crossbow products. The key principal is Hsiu Ching Chang. Known for its dedication to product research, development, and quality, Man Kung serves customers both domestically and internationally.

## Key Features

Man Kung Enterprise Co., Ltd. specializes in the design and manufacturing of archery products, focusing on crossbows, archery bows, blowguns, knives, slingshots, and archery-related accessories. Established in 1988, the company is recognized as one of the world's largest manufacturers in the archery industry. Key capabilities include experienced in-house research and development, continuous innovation to meet market demands, and a commitment to high product quality that has earned widespread international trust. Man Kung positions itself as a one-stop supplier for all archery and related equipment needs.
