## Basic Information

EK ARCHERY, originating from Taiwan under Poe Lang Enterprise Co., Ltd., has more than 30 years of experience in the archery industry. The brand was officially launched for global markets in 2015, rapidly establishing popularity across Europe and internationally. EK ARCHERY is recognized for pioneering advancements such as the world’s first semi-automatic repeating crossbow. Their products are distributed in over 20 countries, including the USA and Europe, and are known for reliability, durability, and continuous innovation. The company maintains a commitment to quality control and end-user feedback, with manufacturing processes blending traditional expertise and modern technology.

## Key Features

EK ARCHERY is recognized for its innovation and functionality in crossbow and bow designs, aiming to provide high-quality, versatile archery products for hunting, sport shooting, and self-defense. Key features and capabilities include:

- A broad product range covering pistol crossbows, compound crossbows, repeating crossbows, traditional bows, and archery accessories. Each category is designed for specific applications such as hunting, tactical use, and target practice.
- Innovative multi-shot systems, compact tactical designs, and repeating crossbows that offer rapid-fire and ease-of-use capabilities ([EK ARCHERY Crossbows](https://www.ek-archery.com/products/crossbow/)).
- Emphasis on safety—every product integrates features such as automatic dry-fire prevention systems for user protection ([Hattila - EK ARCHERY](https://www.hattila.com/en/209-crossbow-ek-archery)).
- High standards of quality control, CNC machining, and use of advanced materials for exceptional durability, performance, and lifespan ([Outfit4Events - EK ARCHERY](https://www.outfit4events.com/eur/category/1017-brand-ek-archery/)).

- Pioneering defensive and rapid-loading crossbow technology, such as lever-action cocking and top-loading magazine systems ([Gun Trade World](https://www.guntradeworld.com/ek-archery’s-reveals-defensive-capabilities)).
