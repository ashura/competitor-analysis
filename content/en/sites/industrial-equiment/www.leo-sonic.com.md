## Basic Information

LEO Ultrasonic Co., LTD (力鴻實業有限公司) was founded in 1987 in Taiwan and is based in New Taipei City. The company is led by representative Chen Zhi-Mou. With over 30 years of experience, LEO Ultrasonic focuses on the production of ultrasonic cleaning machines and related automation equipment. Their products are ISO 9001 and CE certified and are exported to Europe, America, Southeast Asia, and other international markets. LEO Ultrasonic also engages in eco-friendly solvent research, cleaning process planning, and automation equipment, serving a wide range of industrial needs.

## Key Features

LEO Ultrasonic Co., LTD specializes in the design and manufacture of ultrasonic cleaning equipment, offering a wide range of products and services for various industries. Key features and main capabilities include:

1. **Broad Product Line:** Tabletop/benchtop digital ultrasonic cleaners, industrial ultrasonic cleaners, ultrasonic vegetable and fruit washers, ultrasonic sink dishwashers, ultrasonic mold cleaners, and industrial ovens.
2. **Custom Solutions:** Comprehensive OEM/ODM services, enabling custom machine design and manufacturing based on client requirements, including whole plant automated cleaning systems.
3. **Quality and Experience:** High-quality products, rigorous quality control, and decades of expertise in ultrasonic cleaning technology.
4. **Industry Applications:** Supports a diversity of sectors with standardized and highly customized solutions.
5. **International Reach:** Products are exported worldwide, leveraging advanced manufacturing, skilled teams, and compliance with international standards.
