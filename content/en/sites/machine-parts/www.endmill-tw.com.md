## Basic Information

Founded in 1996, JIN LI CHENG CUTTING TOOL CO., LTD. is based in Lukang Township, Changhua County, Taiwan, at No. 150, Lane 2, Section 5, Zhanglu Road. The company focuses on the design, manufacturing, research, and development of tungsten carbide cutting tools such as end mills, drills, and reamers. It serves industries including aerospace, medical, automotive, and mold manufacturing with high-quality tools and technical expertise. The company is committed to meeting diverse and demanding processing requirements by providing innovative products and dependable service.

## Key Features

JIN LI CHENG CUTTING TOOL CO., LTD. specializes in the design and manufacture of high-precision CNC cutting tools, particularly carbide end mills, drills, and reamers. Established in 1996, the company focuses on R&D and production for industries such as aerospace, medical, automotive, and mold manufacturing. They offer a diverse product range, including specialized end mill series (e.g., JA, JB, AP4 for nickel alloy), with features like unequal helix angles and R-shaped grooves to reduce chatter, improve rigidity, and enhance chip removal. JIN LI CHENG emphasizes large in-stock inventories and innovative process development to continually enhance cutting tool performance from raw material to final product.

## Featured Products

JIN LI CHENG CUTTING TOOL CO., LTD. specializes in high performance carbide end mills and a wide range of cutting tools. Their feature products include:

1. **AP Aerospace Cutting Tools Series:** Engineered for aerospace machining applications requiring high precision and performance.
2. **SUPER End Mills:** Designed for superior cutting performance and durability across various materials.
3. **JA Ultra Fine Carbide Series End Mills:** Ultra-fine grain carbide for excellent wear resistance and accurate machining.
4. **JB Micro Grain Carbide End Mills:** High strength micro-grain carbide for tough cutting tasks.
5. **JC Carbide Aluminum Series End Mills:** Optimized specifically for aluminum and light alloy processing.
6. **JD Carbide High Speed Hard Material End Mills Series:** Tailored for hard materials with high speed cutting capabilities.
7. **JE Carbide Special Cutters Series:** Customized solutions for specific or difficult machining requirements.
8. **JF Carbide Thread Milling Tools Series End Mills:** Precision thread milling including prototypes and mass production.
9. **JG Carbide Tools for Compound Lathe Series:** Suitable for CNC and traditional lathe operations.
10. **JH & JI Brazed Carbide Cutters:** Durable options for varied general and specialized applications.
11. **JJ Carbide Drill Series:** High precision solid carbide drills for a wide material range.
12. **JL Carbide Slitting Saws Series:** Precise slitting solutions with robust carbide construction.

## Technical Strength

JIN LI CHENG CUTTING TOOL CO., LTD. demonstrates significant technical strength and competitiveness in the field of high-precision cutting tools, particularly for challenging aerospace applications. Key highlights include:

- The company specializes in the development and manufacturing of high-precision cutting tools using tungsten carbide as the core material, prized for its hardness and wear resistance.
- Proprietary ultra-wear-resistant diamond-like carbon (DLC) coating is applied to tools, enabling high-temperature resistance, cutting stability, and extended tool life—addressing the machining challenges posed by hard-to-cut aerospace materials like titanium alloys.
- Their AP series products are specifically designed for the aerospace sector, significantly improving machining precision, processing efficiency, and tool longevity, and meeting the industry’s strict quality and reliability standards.
- The company emphasizes customized solutions, leveraging nearly 30 years of experience to deliver tailored technical support and professional machining tool solutions across aerospace, medical, automotive, and mold-making industries.
- Ongoing investment in R&D focuses on innovative tool geometry, coating technologies, and materials science to further enhance competitiveness and sustain a leadership role in precision aerospace machining.
