## Basic Information

Founded in 1996, Li Hsing Precision Tool Co., Ltd. is located in Wurih District, Taichung City, Taiwan (No.33-1, Ranze Lane, Syuetian Rd., Wurih Dist., Taichung City 41451, Taiwan). The company specializes in the design, production, and global supply of solid carbide cutting tools, particularly end mills, ball nose end mills, corner radius end mills, and tools for aluminum alloy processing. Emphasizing quality, innovation, and customer service, Li Hsing has built a strong reputation domestically and internationally, exporting to over 30 countries across Asia, Europe, the Middle East, the Americas, and Oceania. Their product range supports various industries needing precision machining and high-performance tools.

## Featured Products

Li Hsing Precision Tool Co., Ltd. specializes in manufacturing a wide range of solid carbide end mills and related precision cutting tools. Their feature products include:

1. **Corner Radius End Mills** – Designed for operations requiring radius edges for improved tool life and finishing.
2. **Ball Nose End Mills** – Suitable for 3D contouring and machining complex surfaces.
3. **Square End Mills** – General-purpose mills used for slotting, profiling, and plunging.
4. **Aluminum Alloy Processing End Mills** – Specially engineered for machining aluminum, acrylic, and plastic materials.
5. **Roughing End Mills** – Ideal for rapid material removal and roughing-out operations.
6. **Irregular Helix Flutes Stainless Steel High Performance End Mills** – High performance for stainless steel processing.
7. **Micro Diameter End Mills (Square & Ball Nose)** – For precision micro-machining applications.
8. **Taper Flute End Mills** – Used for mold and die industries and complex geometries.
9. **Precision Reamers** – Provide accurate hole finishing with tight tolerances.
10. **60° Center Drills** – Used in centering workpieces in turning or milling.
11. **Internal Partial Profile 60° Carbide Thread Mill** – For precision internal thread milling.

These products cover applications such as aerospace, medical, die and mold, and precision industries. For detailed descriptions and images of each product category, you can visit their product overview pages here: [https://www.li-hsing.com/product.html](https://www.li-hsing.com/product.html)
