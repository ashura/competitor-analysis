## Basic Information

Founded in 1999, SHYI CHANG ENTERPRISE CO., LTD. focuses on producing a wide range of precision gears and machining parts. The company emphasizes high quality and accuracy, utilizing advanced gear grinding, hobbing, and shaping machines from Switzerland, Germany, and Japan to enhance product quality and production efficiency. Its products are applied across automotive and motorcycle industries, electric vehicles, machine tool transmission mechanisms, speed reduction mechanisms, agricultural machinery, and customized gear manufacturing.
