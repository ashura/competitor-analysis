## Basic Information

NSK Europe Ltd. serves as the European arm of NSK Ltd., a globally recognized manufacturer headquartered in Tokyo, Japan. Established in 1916, NSK has grown to operate worldwide, with NSK Europe advancing the group's presence across Europe. The company delivers high-quality solutions including bearings, linear components, and engine parts, supporting both industrial and automotive markets. NSK Europe's headquarters are in Maidenhead, Berkshire, United Kingdom, and the organization operates multiple locations across Europe. In the fiscal year 2024, NSK Europe employed 2,976 people and reported sales exceeding €587 million. The parent company, NSK Ltd., is renowned for pioneering the development of bearings in Japan and expanding its technological offerings globally.

## Key Features

NSK Europe Ltd. is a leading manufacturer specializing in bearings, linear motion products, and mechatronic solutions. Its major divisions include Industrial Bearings & Linear Components, Automotive Bearings & Modules, and Steering Systems. The company’s product portfolio covers ball bearings, cylindrical, tapered, and spherical roller bearings, thrust bearings, super precision bearings, mounted units, ball screws, linear guides, and a broad range of mechatronic products. These products serve numerous sectors like agriculture, automotive production, cement, domestic appliances, electrical, food & beverage, industrial gearboxes, handling & automation, injection molding, machine tools, power transmission, mining, construction, railway, semiconductor, medical, steel, metals, and wind energy.

NSK Europe operates production plants in the UK, Germany, and Poland; has multiple sales, technology, and logistics centers across Europe and the Middle East. Key technological areas include tribology (friction control), numerical simulation, materials research, and mechatronics. The company is committed to innovation with comprehensive R&D in application engineering, design, testing, and investigation. NSK Europe has received numerous industry awards from automotive and industrial clients such as Bosch, Continental, Toyota, Volkswagen, and others, highlighting its reputation for quality, innovation, and supply excellence.

## Featured Products

NSK Europe Ltd. offers a wide range of featured products primarily in the field of bearings and linear motion components. The key product offerings are as follows:

### Bearings
(Source: [Bearings](https://www.nskeurope.com/eu-en/products/bearings/))
- **Ball Bearings:** Deep groove ball bearings, angular contact ball bearings, self-aligning ball bearings, thrust ball bearings, SPACEA series for special environments.
- **Roller Bearings:** Cylindrical roller bearings, tapered roller bearings, spherical roller bearings, thrust roller bearings, needle roller bearings.
- **Bearing Units:** J-Line bearing units to JIS standard, Self-Lube ISO standard bearing units, plummer blocks, agri disc hub units.
- **Super Precision Bearings:** Super precision ball bearings (e.g., precision angular contact), super precision roller bearings (cylindrical type), ball screw support bearings.

### Linear Motion Control
(Source: [Linear Motion](https://www.nskeurope.com/eu-en/products/linear/))
- **Linear Products:** Ball screws, linear guides, linear actuators (Monocarriers).
- **Megatorque Motors™:** Direct drive motors for high-precision and high-speed positioning applications.
- **Other Precision Machine Components:** XY tables and related linear/rotating component accessories.

NSK Europe also supplies solutions for special industry requirements such as medical devices and automotive applications.

## Technical Strength

NSK Europe Ltd. demonstrates significant technical strength through its broad product portfolio and advanced R&D activities. The company’s technologies are built on 'Four Core Technologies plus One':

- **Tribology:** Advanced lubrication and surface processing for faster, quieter, and more durable bearings capable of withstanding heavy loads.
- **Numerical Simulation:** Use of computer simulations for rigorous product development and optimization under extreme conditions.
- **Materials:** Ongoing research and innovation in material design, heat treatment, and performance evaluation to enhance bearing functionality and durability.
- **Mechatronics:** Integration of mechanics and electronics for innovations in high-performance motors, control technologies, and precision sensing, playing a vital role in demanding environments and reliability.
- **Manufacturing Engineering:** Adoption of AI, IoT, and smart factory initiatives to optimize production, reduce energy and manpower, and enhance environmental and safety performance.

NSK Europe Ltd. operates multiple R&D centers, technology centers, and production facilities across Europe, supporting diverse industries from automotive to medical devices and wind energy. The company has also received numerous supplier and quality awards from global OEMs, highlighting its leading capabilities in precision, innovation, and customer-oriented engineering.

## Environment / Society / Governance

NSK Europe Ltd. (NSK Group) demonstrates significant investments and active achievements in ESG (Environment, Society, Corporate Governance):

### Environment

- Coverage of ISO 14001 certified sites has consistently exceeded 95%, with zero serious environmental law violations in the past several years.
- Investments in environmental conservation and costs related to environmental activities totaled over 1.5 billion yen in Fiscal Year 2023 (with total conservation costs over 9.6 billion yen).
- Drastic increase in renewable energy usage: from 0.2% (FY2017) to 34.5% (FY2023).
- GHG (Scope 1+2) emissions fell by over 51% from FY2017 levels; aiming for carbon neutrality (Scope 1&2) by 2035.
- Over 99% of waste is recycled (99.2% recycling rate in FY2023), with significant reductions in industrial waste and water usage per unit of sales.
- Biodiversity initiatives include marine and river cleanups, tree planting, and nature observation events.
- 246 environmentally friendly products developed as of FY2023, with a cumulative 2,435,000 tons of CO2 saved through their products.

### Society

- Around 25,632 employees globally (FY2023), with a focus on diversity (19.1% women, 13.5% female managers).
- Extensive occupational health and safety programs, with a low lost time injury frequency rate (0.28 globally in FY2023) and zero fatal accidents.
- Health and wellness initiatives: regular health checks (100% coverage in Japan), stress checks, and Specific Health Guidance, with high participation.
- Employee engagement and development includes 100% union participation for non-management staff in Japan, regular training, internal mobility, and rehiring systems for senior employees.
- Legal employment rates for people with disabilities in Japan exceeded (2.62% vs. 2.3% required).

### Governance

- Board of Directors (as of June 2024): 9 members, 55.6% independent outside directors, 11.1% women.
- Independently-chaired Nomination, Audit, and Compensation Committees; board/committee members have high attendance rates (98-100%).
- Zero serious legal violations, corruption, or competition law breaches in the past five years.
- Active compliance and anti-corruption training, with over 16,000 participants yearly.
- President/CEO pay ratio and employee average salaries are transparently disclosed.
