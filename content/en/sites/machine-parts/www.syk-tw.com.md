## Basic Information
Founded in 1989, Sonyung Industry Co., Ltd. (SYK) is headquartered in Lukang Township, Changhua, Taiwan. The company specializes in designing and manufacturing automation equipment components, with its main products including ball screw support units and servo motor brackets. Initially established as an OEM for precision mechanical parts, SYK later developed its proprietary SYK-branded products, fostering innovation and diversification in its product line.

SYK is recognized for its commitment to stable quality, fast delivery, and reasonable pricing, which has established its reputation among global customers. Their products are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, construction, manufacturing, robotics, semiconductor machinery, CNC processing, 3D printers, medical equipment, and solar panels. SYK supplies actuators, nut brackets, flexible shaft couplings, and numerous other precision transmission components. The company has passed ISO9001 certification, and their products comply with ROHS standards, underscoring its focus on quality and environmental compliance.
