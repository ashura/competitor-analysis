## Basic Information
Lotus Innovation Technology Co., Ltd. (蓮花創新科技股份有限公司) is a Taiwan-based company located at No.7, Sec. 1, Zhongshan Rd., Taiping Dist., Taichung City 411, Taiwan. The company is recognized internationally as a manufacturer of oil mist collectors, oil mist air cleaners, smoke dust air cleaners, and related smart devices for industrial environments. Its products are designed to eliminate harmful oil mist, smoke, and dust from machining processes, thereby enhancing air quality and protecting worker health. Lotus Innovation emphasizes product certification, with compliance to SGS, TTR, and CE standards as well as ISO inspection, ensuring both domestic and global customers of its quality standards. The product lineup serves various machining processes including lathing, milling, grinding, drilling, and special purposes.
