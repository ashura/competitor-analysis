## Basic Information

Luyang Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 1981 and is headquartered at No.68, Keji S. Road, Erlin Township, Changhua County, Taiwan 526-004. The company specializes in the design, manufacturing, and sale of motors, gear reducers, and related components, including AC motors, brushless DC motors, brushed DC motors, gear reducers, ring blowers, and vacuum pumps. Luyang serves both domestic and international customers across regions such as North America, Europe, Japan, India, and Southeast Asia. With ISO 9001-2015 certification and a strong emphasis on quality control, the company operates a flexible production line capable of accommodating OEM and ODM requests and customized products. Luyang has planned and expanded its facilities in the Central Taiwan Science Park to enhance R&D, smart manufacturing, and workplace quality. The company's products are widely applied in machinery, automation equipment, packaging, food processing, medical devices, and more. Core values focus on technological advancement, customer service, and continuous improvement.

## Featured Products

Luyang Technology Co., Ltd. offers a wide range of feature products mainly focused on motors and gear reducers, including:

1. **Brushless Motors:** Both AC and DC input types, with available models for 30W, 50W, 60W, 100W, and 120W brushless motor drivers.
2. **AC Small Gear Motors:** Including motor with gear box, induction motors (IK Type-K Series), reversible motors, clutch brake motors, alloy worm motors, assembled and separated speed control motors, and motor with terminal box, as well as compatible bracket stands and controllers.
3. **AC Gear Motors:** Available as horizontal and vertical gear motors, with standard and high ratio variants. Also offered are gear motors specifically for carton sealing machines.
4. **Gearboxes:** Models such as GN Helical GearBox, GU Enhanced Helical GearBox, and Decimal GearBox.
5. **Motor Controllers:** Including the UX Digital Speed Controller, SS Separated Speed Controller, SB Instantaneous Brake Controller, and SSHR Speed & Brake Controller.
6. **Helical Gear Reducers:** Including horizontal and vertical reducer motor plug-ins, and double shaft reducers.
7. **Brushed DC Motors:** Offering permanent-magnet brushed DC motors in various frame sizes (D05/D06/D07/D08/D10/D12/D18).
8. **Ring Blowers:** For side channel applications.
9. **Vacuum Pumps:** Such as oil-lubricated rotary vane vacuum pumps.
10. **Customized Products:** Including custom motors (such as BLDC for hand tools) and gearboxes for OEM & ODM solutions.

Applications of these products cover automated machinery and equipment, conveyor systems, packaging machines, food machinery, power tools, filling and labeling machines, and medical equipment.
