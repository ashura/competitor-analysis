## Basic Information
TANSHING ACCURATE INDUSTRIAL CO., LTD. was established in 1995 and is headquartered in Taichung City, Taiwan. The company focuses on the research, development, and manufacture of high-precision CNC rotary tables, indexing tables, and auto pallet changers. Tanshing emphasizes technological innovation and strict quality control throughout its production process. The company has imported advanced facilities from the USA, Germany, Switzerland, and Japan to ensure top-tier manufacturing capabilities. Tanshing’s products are widely used and have earned a global reputation for quality and reliability.

## Key Features
Tanshing specializes in the design and manufacture of high-precision CNC rotary tables, index tables, and auto pallet changers primarily for use in machining centers and special purpose machines. Notable features and main capabilities include:

1. Innovative, patented dual lead worm drive system offering high torque, accuracy, and durability.
2. Complete product lineup, including 4th and 5th axis rotary tables, tilting rotary tables, hydraulic indexing tables, and related metalworking accessories.
3. Extensive R&D capabilities enabling custom solutions, rapid development, and continual innovation in speed and load capacity.
4. In-house manufacturing with 90% of parts machined internally, ensuring stringent quality control and precision.
5. Certification to AQSR ISO-9001 (U.S.A.), reflecting strong commitment to quality management.
6. Products widely recognized and adopted in industries such as automotive, aerospace, electronics, medical devices, and mold manufacturing, both domestically and globally.

## Featured Products
According to the official website of TANSHING ACCURATE INDUSTRIAL CO., LTD., the company's featured products include:

1. **CNC Rotary Table:** Precision rotary tables suitable for the 4th and 5th axis machining, utilized for complex cutting processes.
2. **CNC Index Table:** Designed for high-precision indexing and positioning on CNC machines.
3. **Hydraulic Index Table:** Hydraulic-driven solutions for repeatable and accurate indexing tasks.
4. **CNC Manual Tilting Rotary Table:** Enables angular positioning for multi-axis machining applications.
5. **CNC Tilting Rotary Table:** Automated tilting mechanisms to achieve multiple angles in machining processes.
6. **Auto Pallet Changer:** Systems to automate pallet exchanging on machining centers to improve productivity.
7. **Rotary Spindle Head:** Rotational head units providing additional flexibility in machining.
8. **Tailstock:** Supporting components for workpiece stability during rotation or machining.
9. **Optional Accessories:** Complementary components and upgrades for customization, such as encoders and clamping systems.
