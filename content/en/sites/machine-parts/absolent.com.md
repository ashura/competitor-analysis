## Basic Information

Absolent AB was founded in 1993 in Sweden by Thomas Lindblom, Joakim Westh, and Dan Holmberg. The company quickly became a market leader in oil mist filtration on its domestic market. Absolent provides air filtration solutions that clean contaminated air at the source, particularly in industrial settings such as metalworking, die casting, cold forming, and welding. Their product portfolio includes oil mist collectors, ambient air cleaners, and dust collectors, serving a variety of industries globally. Headquartered in Gothenburg, Sweden, Absolent AB is part of the Absolent Air Care Group and is recognized for its contributions to improving air quality and workplace safety in industrial environments.

## Key Features

Absolent AB specializes in industrial air filtration solutions, focusing on the removal of oil mist, oil smoke, dust, and fumes in production environments. Core capabilities include designing and manufacturing both small, machine-mounted filters and large, centralized systems for various industrial settings.

Key features include:
- Compact, lightweight, and high-performance filtration products, suitable for tight spaces and direct machine installation.
- Innovation with patented systems like CleanChange™ for safe, tool-free maintenance, and True Downflow™ technology to maximize airflow efficiency.
- Over 25 years of expertise and a strong environmental commitment.
- Customized solutions to protect both indoor and outdoor environments by extracting contaminants at the source.
- International operations with subsidiaries in Sweden, the US, Germany, France, and China.

## Featured Products

Absolent AB offers a wide range of air filtration solutions for industrial settings. Main product groups include:

1. **A•mist Oil Mist Filter** – Systems designed to remove oil mist from the air, commonly used with machining and manufacturing equipment.
2. **A•erity Oil Mist Filter** – Advanced oil mist filters for more efficient and flexible air cleaning.
3. **AD Dust & Fume Extraction** – Filters and extraction systems for dust and fumes, suitable for various industrial applications.
4. **AE Oil Smoke Extraction** – Solutions focused on extracting oil smoke to maintain a clean environment in workshops and plants.
5. **AW Machine Mounted Oil Mist Filter** – Compact, machine-mounted filters for immediate mist extraction at the source.
6. **A•line Oil Smoke Filter** – Specialized for filtering oil smoke in heavy-duty environments.
7. **A•smoke Oil Smoke Filter** – Filters for the removal of fine oil smoke particles from the air.
8. **A•smart IoT-solution** – Digital and IoT-enabled solutions for monitoring and optimizing filter and air quality performance.
9. **Accessories** – Various add-ons and support components to enhance filtration system performance.

Applications include heat treatment, forming/pressing, die casting, grinding, food processing, rubber & plastics, textiles, and machining.
