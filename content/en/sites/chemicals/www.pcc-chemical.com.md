## Basic Information
Pan-Continental Chemical Co., Ltd. was established in 1979 and is headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan, with its registered address in Taipei. The company manufactures a range of inorganic chemical compounds, particularly zinc and copper derivatives, including zinc oxide, active zinc oxide, zinc carbonate, nano zinc oxide, copper oxide, and copper carbonate. Pan-Continental Chemical Co., Ltd. operates six manufacturing plants located in Taiwan, China, and Vietnam, catering to global industrial chemical supply needs. The company is led by its representative, 洪名立, and has a registered capital of NT$456,000,000.

## Key Features
Pan-Continental Chemical Co., Ltd., established in 1979, is a leading manufacturer and supplier of zinc and copper compounds with over 40 years of experience. The company specializes in high-quality zinc oxide (including active zinc oxide and nano zinc oxide), zinc carbonate, copper oxide, copper carbonate, and zinc chloride, among other specialty chemicals. Pan-Continental’s products serve a wide range of industries such as rubber, plastics, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, electronics, and paper.

**Key capabilities and features include:**
- Advanced production methods: Uses direct, indirect, and wet processes to produce zinc oxide for various industry requirements, ensuring product quality and purity.
- High purity and consistent quality: Rigorous quality control ensures suitability for sensitive applications like electronics and pharmaceuticals.
- Wide product portfolio: Offers diverse zinc and copper compounds that can be customized for specific needs and applications.
- Environmental commitment: Active zinc oxide CS AZO is produced through clean and sustainable methods. All products meet international standards (REACH, SVHC, and RoHS compliance).

## Environment / Society / Governance
Pan-Continental Chemical Co., Ltd. has made significant investments and achievements in ESG areas (environment, society, and corporate governance).

**Environmental Achievements:**
- Carbon Reduction: Upgrades like boiler replacements have reduced annual carbon emissions by 1,224 tons, equivalent to removing over 250 passenger vehicles per year.
- Power Reduction: Installation of solar panels and high-efficiency equipment has cut electricity usage by over 500,000 kWh annually—enough to power 138 average Taiwanese households.
- Green Products: The company’s Active ZnO (CS AZO) emits only 20% of the carbon compared to conventional zinc oxide production.
- Factory upgrades include the adoption of solar energy, high-efficiency heat exchangers and pumps, and conversion to LED lighting to minimize electricity consumption.

**Certifications (Environmental & Governance):**
- ISO 14067 (carbon footprint certification for zinc products)
- Sedex (supply chain transparency and ethical data exchange covering labor, health & safety, environmental impact, and business ethics)
- UL 2809 (recycled content validation for copper oxide and active zinc oxide)
- ZDHC Level 3 (highest level of chemical safety and hazardous substance reduction for zinc oxide and zinc carbonate)

**Social & Governance Initiatives:**
- Sedex certification provides ethical supply chain transparency and undergoes audits regarding labor rights, health, safety, and business ethics, building trust with global clients by reducing risks from non-compliant suppliers.

By integrating eco-friendly innovations and third-party certifications, Pan-Continental Chemical supports its partners' regulatory and market needs while advancing sustainability across the chemical industry supply chain.
