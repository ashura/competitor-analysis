## Basic Information

Arneplant was founded in 1999 by the Ruiz brothers in Arnedo, La Rioja, Spain. The company specializes in the production of insoles, foams, and microfibers for footwear, providing innovative, breathable, and comfortable solutions. Over the years, Arneplant has become a benchmark in the European footwear component industry. The company operates both in Spain and internationally, with a production facility in Vietnam established to better serve global markets. As of recent records, Arneplant employs around 80 people and maintains a strong focus on R&D and sustainable manufacturing practices.

## Technical Strength

Arneplant demonstrates significant technical strength and competitiveness through full control of its production process, allowing it to offer customized, exclusive products for demanding sectors such as work, casual, sports, health, and orthopedic footwear.
