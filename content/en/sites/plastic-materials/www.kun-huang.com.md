## Basic Information

Kun Huang Enterprise Co., Ltd. (坤璜企業股份有限公司) is based in Miaoli County, Taiwan (No.9, Ming Chuan Rd., Tongluo Township, Miaoli County 366, Taiwan) and specializes in the production and innovation of polyurethane foam materials—such as open cell foam, high-density foam sheets, composite foams, and custom formulations. The company emphasizes eco-friendly processes, reducing energy use and carbon emissions, and complying with international environmental standards. With over 60 years of expertise in PU foam manufacturing and technology, Kun Huang serves diverse markets like sports and leisure, military and industry, footwear, electronic protection, rehabilitation, construction, and transportation. They offer comprehensive services covering R&D, design, prototyping, QC, and mass production.

## Environment / Society / Governance

Kun Huang Enterprise Co., Ltd. has shown notable commitment to ESG (environment, society, and corporate governance) principles. The company consistently works to reduce carbon emissions by utilizing pre-consumer foam and converting various agricultural and ocean waste (such as pineapple leaves, coffee grounds, and oyster shell powder) into special bio-based materials for their products. This initiative not only prevents environmental pollution that can occur when these wastes are burned or discarded, but also aligns with the company's promotion of the 3R concept—recycle, reduce, reuse. Through these practices, Kun Huang aims to contribute to societal well-being and environmental protection while making cities cleaner and better.
*Source: [ESG Story](https://www.kun-huang.com/en/news/the-story-of-esg-from-kun-huang)*
