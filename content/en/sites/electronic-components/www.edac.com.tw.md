## Basic Information

Established in 1998, **EDAC POWER ELECTRONICS CO., LTD. ** is a prominent Taiwanese manufacturer focused on global standard and custom power supply solutions, including AC/DC switching power supplies, USB Type-C adapters, DC/DC converters, battery chargers, LED power supplies, and both open-frame and enclosed high-power modules, within a wide design range from 4.5W to 2500W. Headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan, with additional production and R&D centers in Suzhou, China, the company serves various sectors such as ICT, medical, e-bikes, lighting, and industrial markets. EDAC POWER ELECTRONICS is renowned for its dedication to quality, innovation, and customer-centric values, with its plants holding ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 13485, ISO 14064, and ISO 14067 certifications, reflecting strong environmental and product quality commitments. The company employs approximately 500 people.
