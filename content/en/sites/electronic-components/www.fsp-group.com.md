## Basic Information

FSP TECHNOLOGY INC. (全漢企業), established in 1993 and headquartered in Taoyuan, Taiwan, specializes in the manufacturing, processing, and trading of electronic power supplies and components. The company is recognized for its expertise in producing AC/DC power supplies, battery chargers, PV inverters, and open-frame power supplies. FSP serves multiple sectors with products such as power supplies for consumer electronics, battery chargers, solid-state lighting units, edge computing products, and medical devices. The company offers both OEM and ODM services, emphasizes green power technologies and energy efficiency, and maintains a global presence supported by strong R&D capabilities and extended logistics networks.
