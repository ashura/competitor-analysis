## Basic Information

Brady People ID, originally founded as Comprehensive Identification Products (CIPI) in 1966, is headquartered in Massachusetts, United States. The company specializes in identification, safety, and security solutions, and holds patents in medical equipment and related software. CIPI was acquired by Brady Corporation (NYSE: BRC), a global manufacturer of workplace identification and safety solutions based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Following the acquisition, CIPI merged with JAM Plastics to become Brady People ID. Brady People ID is recognized for its broad range of identification products and serves as a key brand within Brady Corporation.

## Featured Products

Brady People ID offers a comprehensive range of identification, security, and promotional products serving markets such as public relations, marketing, healthcare, and original equipment manufacturing. Their primary products include:

1. **Badge Holders**: Multiple materials and sizes for conventions and large meetings.
2. **Badge Reels**: Various styles and colors, customizable for branding.
3. **Code® Barcode Readers**: Scans barcodes of any size and condition with fast decoding.
4. **Components**: Lanyard end fittings, breakaways, clips, chains, etc., for assembling ID products.
5. **ID, Access, and Role Recognition Cards**: Standard ID cards, custom credentials, oversized cards, access cards, and Badge Buddies.
6. **Lanyards**: Several styles, customizable for promotion or schools.
7. **Luggage Accessories**: Luggage straps, loops, and customizable tag holders.
8. **Printers, Software & Supplies**: Card printers, supplies, and photo ID software.
9. **Strap Clips**: Affordable solutions for attaching badges.
10. **Visitor Management Products**: Patented TEMPbadge™ expiring badges and visitor management software like TEMPbadge VMS and PassagePoint™.
11. **Wristwear**: Wrist coils and UBand™ contactless wristbands with proximity card functionality.
