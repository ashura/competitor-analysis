## Basic Information

NIYOK SEALING PARTS CO., LTD. is based in Lukang Township, Changhua, Taiwan, and boasts over 40 years of professional experience in the sealing engineering field. The company manufactures seals and oil seal products using advanced 3D CAD software and modern facilities, serving a variety of industrial and automotive sectors. NIYOK’s core expertise includes the design and manufacture of high-quality rubber and sealing components for global customers.

## Featured Products

NIYOK specializes in high-quality sealing and rubber components for automotive and industrial applications. Featured products include:

1. **Shock Absorber Seal & Cover:** Prevents fluid leakage in shock absorbers.
2. **Valve Stem Seal:** Controls engine valve leaks with low-friction materials.
3. **Power Steering Oil Seal:** Suited for automotive power steering.
4. **Brake Caliper Seal:** Piston seal and dust boot types for effective brake performance.
5. **Transmission Seal:** Used in automatic transmissions for superior sealing.
6. **O Ring:** Customizable for diverse pressure and environmental needs; for dynamic/static use.
7. **X Ring:** Double-lobed design for enhanced sealing.
8. **V Ring:** All-rubber, easy installation for rotary shafts.
9. **Gasket:** Strictly inspected for reliable sealing.
10. **Shake-proof Gasket:** Designed for motorcycles; available in standard sizes.
11. **Automobile Gaskets:** For cylinder and other automotive applications.
12. **Special Synthetic Rubber Gasket:** Corrosion- and heat-resistant, maintaining elasticity.
