## Basic Information

Aok Valve Stem Seals Ltd. was established in 1995 and is based in Nantou City, Nantou County, Taiwan. The company specializes in manufacturing automotive rubber oil seals, valve stem seals, bonded seals, O-rings, bushings, absorbers, and precision custom rubber-metal parts. AOK offers OEM and ODM services and puts significant emphasis on product quality, research, and development. Their products serve automotive and industrial applications worldwide.

## Key Features

Aok Valve Stem Seals Ltd., established in 1995 and located in Nantou, Taiwan, is a professional manufacturer specializing in the production of valve stem seals, bonded seals (sealing washers), large size O-rings, large size oil seals, bushings, and precision customized rubber molded parts. The company provides OEM and ODM services especially for the automotive sector, delivering both standard and custom-designed sealing solutions.
