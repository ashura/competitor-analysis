## Basic Information

New V-Key Technology Co., Ltd., established in 1989 and headquartered at 10F., No. 965, Zhongzheng Rd., Zhonghe Dist., New Taipei City 23544, Taiwan, is a prominent Taiwanese manufacturer of medical cables, connectors, accessories, and cable assemblies. The company focuses on OEM and ODM production for medical applications, including ECG/EKG/EEG/EMG cables, nurse call cords, electrodes, and custom cable solutions for diagnostic equipment such as ultrasound and ECG machines.
