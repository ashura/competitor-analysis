## Basic Information
Cambus Corporation, established in 1997, is a privately owned company headquartered at 5F., No. 98, Tung-Hsin Rd., Sec. 2, Taichung, 40857, Taiwan. The company specializes in engineering and manufacturing custom solutions for medical cable assemblies, plastic components, connectors, wire harnesses, and plastic injection moldings. With a robust supplier network in both Taiwan and China, Cambus offers services such as tooling, stamping, CNC machining parts, SMT processing, and PCB manufacturing. The firm is recognized for its commitment to quality and customer-oriented engineering support in the medical device sector.

## Key Features
Cambus Corporation is a leading manufacturer with over 20 years of expertise, specializing in OEM and ODM solutions for medical, electronics, automation, and military industries. Their core capabilities include design and manufacture of custom medical cable assemblies, medical connectors (such as ECG, EKG, Redel, and Lemo types), and precision injection-molded plastic components. Cambus provides end-to-end one-stop outsourcing services, precision CNC lathe metal parts, electronics parts, and offers custom solutions for client-specific requests. The company maintains strict adherence to international standards (such as IEC 60601-1), and incorporates safety features like locking mechanisms and color-coding in medical connectors. Their products are widely used in medical devices (e.g., sensors, monitors, imaging systems, surgical instruments), as well as for military connectors like waterproof D-sub connectors. They can accommodate both low and high-volume production with custom tooling, PCB assembly, and electronics sourcing services.
