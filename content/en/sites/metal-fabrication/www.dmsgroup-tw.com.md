## Basic Information
DMS Group provides one-stop custom CNC machining services from rapid prototyping to mass production, delivering CNC milling and turning parts tailored to customer specifications. Their operations include advanced 3-axis and 5-axis milling, as well as turning of various metals and plastics for a wide spectrum of industries such as automotive, marine, RC cars, telecommunications, and more. DMS also supplies aluminum and magnesium alloy extrusion components with tight tolerances and heat treatments. The company emphasizes integrity, craftsmanship, and long-term partnerships in their business philosophy. Their offerings span OEM and ODM manufacturing, private labeling, and assembly services, designed to support client projects from concept to finished product.

## Key Features
DMS Group is a Taiwan-based OEM/ODM manufacturer specializing in high-precision CNC machining, metal cutting, pipe bending, welding, and surface finishing. Their key features and capabilities include:

- **One-stop custom metal manufacturing:** From prototyping through mass production, including product assembly, quality inspection, and global shipping/logistics.

- **Product examples:** Recumbent trikes, custom-made wheelchairs, handcycle accessories, mobility scooters, cargo trailers, e-bike conversion kits, folding walkers, adaptive bike equipment, and stainless steel coffee gear.
- **Quality and value:** All products are ISO-standard QC checked; the company promises never to compromise on quality, and offers competitive, reasonable pricing and full customization according to client preferences.

## Technical Strength
DMS Group demonstrates a high level of technical strength and competitiveness in precision manufacturing, particularly in CNC-turned and milled components using advanced Swiss-type and Japan-type auto-feed bar turning. Their extensive capabilities include OEM/ODM product realization, CNC milling (3-axis, 4-axis, 5-axis), aluminum extrusion, metal forging, welding, pipe and sheet metal bending, and aluminum die casting. DMS Group is equipped with up to 20-axis CNC machining, allowing for the production of extremely complex and high-tolerance parts, with tolerances down to ±0.005 mm. Their quality control follows ISO 9001-certified standards, providing clients with inspection certificates and detailed reports. The company serves demanding industries such as automotive, robotics, medical devices, marine, and consumer electronics. Their technical strengths also include rapid prototyping, material flexibility (metals and engineering plastics), and advanced surface treatments for durability in harsh environments (e.g., marine hardware). DMS Group leverages Taiwan’s CNC and precision engineering ecosystem, blending rigorous process discipline with flexible, customer-centric partnership models. Their ongoing investments in advanced machinery and automation ensure they stay at the forefront of industrial innovation and competitiveness.
