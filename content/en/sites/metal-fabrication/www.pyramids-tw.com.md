## Basic Information
Pyramids Technology Corp. is a privately held corporation founded in 1994 as a CAD and CAM dealer in New Taipei City, Taiwan. The company has evolved over nearly 30 years into a diversified supplier of cutting-edge engineering and manufacturing resources, operating facilities in Taiwan and China with a total land size of 173,000 square feet and over 300 employees. Pyramids offers one-stop OEM solutions from product manufacturing analysis to delivery, covering prototyping, plastic processing, metal processing, decorating, and assembly. Their business focuses on worldwide markets, particularly US and EU, with long-term partnerships with well-known enterprises.
