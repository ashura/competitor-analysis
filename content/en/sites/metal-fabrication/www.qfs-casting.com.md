## Basic Information
Qiao Fu Shen Casting is a vertically integrated cast steel foundry located in Taichung City, Taiwan. The company has over 20 years of operation and specializes in providing comprehensive casting services from design engineering to precision machining. Their production capacity ranges from 5 kilograms to 5,000 kilograms per piece, catering to both prototype and mass production needs. The foundry offers custom production cycles adaptable to market demands, including single prototypes and orders exceeding 1,000 pieces. [Source: https://www.qfs-casting.com/about-us](https://www.qfs-casting.com/about-us)
