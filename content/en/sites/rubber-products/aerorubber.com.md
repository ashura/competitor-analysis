## Basic Information

Aero Rubber Company®, Inc., founded in 1973 and based in Tinley Park, Illinois, USA, is a family-run business that manufactures custom rubber components, such as tubing, sheeting, seals, and gaskets. The company is ISO 9001:2015 certified and offers fabrication services like die cutting, water-jet and lathe cutting, slitting, and vulcanized splicing. It is known for supplying high-volume, custom-manufactured rubber parts and industrial rubber bands to a variety of B2B clients. Over fifty years, it has built a reputation for consultative engineering support and timely completion of projects, providing solutions for a range of industrial applications.

## Key Features

Aero Rubber Company®, Inc. is an ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturer specializing in custom, high-volume rubber products. Their core capabilities include the manufacturing of molded, extruded, and fabricated rubber components (such as tubing, sheeting, seals, gaskets, bands, hose, and sleeves) from a wide variety of specification-grade and hard-to-source elastomers.
