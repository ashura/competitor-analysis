## Basic Information
JHAO YANG RUBBER (VN) CO., LTD. is a subsidiary of Jhao Yang Rubber Industrial Co., Ltd., which is based in Taiwan and operates a manufacturing facility in Vietnam. The company specializes in the design, production, and supply of custom-molded rubber and silicone products, including automotive parts, gaskets, seals, and liquid silicone components. Certified to IATF 16949 and ISO 9001 standards, they supply to industries such as automotive, electronics, medical, appliances, construction, and consumer products. They offer various molding technologies like injection, compression, and transfer molding. The company is recognized for its commitment to high-quality manufacturing and customer service.
