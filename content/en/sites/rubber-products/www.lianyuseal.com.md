## Basic Information

Established in 1978 (some sources cite 1979), LIAN YU OIL SEAL ENTERPRISE CO., LTD. (亮宇油封企業股份有限公司) is a professional manufacturer of oil seals for vehicles and various machinery. Headquartered in Taiwan, the company focuses on designing and producing a wide range of oil seals—including rubber and mechanical seals—tailored for industries such as automotive, shipbuilding, petrochemical, hydraulics, pneumatics, and food processing. LIAN YU emphasizes honest operations, innovation, and quality, aiming to provide specialized, durable sealing solutions to meet diverse client requirements.
