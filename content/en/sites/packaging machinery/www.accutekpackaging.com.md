## Basic Information

Founded in 1989 by Edward Chocholek and his sons, Accutek Packaging Equipment Company, Inc. is recognized as one of the premier privately owned packaging machinery manufacturers in the United States. The company specializes in designing and producing comprehensive turnkey packaging solutions, with an extensive product range including filling machines, capping machines, labeling machines, conveyors, and other equipment. Accutek's operations began in Oceanside, California, later expanding to a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Vista, California, with additional locations in Texas and Florida. Their equipment can be customized to meet unique product requirements across various industries.

## Key Features

Accutek Packaging Equipment Company, Inc. is one of the largest privately held packaging machinery manufacturers in the United States, specializing in complete turnkey packaging solutions. Key features and main capabilities include:

- **Comprehensive range of equipment:** Filling machines, capping machines, labeling machines, conveyors, and full packaging systems that can be custom-tailored to unique product needs.

- **Industry commitment:** Focuses on optimizing line efficiency (e.g., minimizing changeover times, automatic torque management for capping machines) and innovative product design (showcased at major industry expos).
- **Strong service and support:** Provides technical documentation, instruction manuals, exploded diagrams, and robust customer support.

## Environment / Society / Governance

Accutek Packaging Equipment Company, Inc. has made several investments and achievements in environmental sustainability (E in ESG):

1. **Solar Energy Investments:**
   Between 2014 and 2015, the company installed solar panels with micro inverters and Cool White reflective roofing material at its California facilities. In total, 386 panels producing 300 watts each were installed at one location, and 245 panels at another, together generating over 173,525 kWh of power annually.

2. **Energy Efficiency Upgrades:**
   Starting in 2012, Accutek began a complete transition to LED lighting across all four facilities, replacing fluorescent and sodium halide lamps. This upgrade was noted to significantly reduce power consumption, improve return on investment, and enhance workplace conditions for employees.

3. **Product Innovation for Sustainability:**
   Since 1996, Accutek has actively worked each year to reduce the power consumption of every machine sold, helping customers improve their energy efficiency.

4. **Recycling Initiatives:**
   The company established a recycling program at its inception in 1989, recycling everything from office paper to waste oil.
