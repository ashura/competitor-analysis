## Key Features

American Canning Machines specializes in compact, fully-automated beverage canning equipment designed for small to mid-sized producers. Key features include:

- **Ultra-compact size:** Tabletop-sized AT-1 Filler/Seamer fits on a cart for mobility (24” x 40” footprint).
- **Flexible can support:** Handles any standard can body (8oz to 19.2oz), including standard, sleek, and slim.
- **Speed:** Consistently fills and seams 8–12 cans per minute.
- **Full automation:** Single-operator, intuitive interface, easy operation for all experience levels.
- **Efficient filling:** Atmospheric filling with advanced pre-purge and underlid gassing for reduced oxygen and waste.
- **Quality construction:** Stainless steel fill-head, rotary lid applicator, servo-driven seamer, upgraded durable materials.
- **Integrated features:** Mini in-feed/out-feed table (22 can capacity), post-fill rinse, wash-down rated design.
- **Easy installation:** Ships ready to use, designed and manufactured in Austin, TX, USA.

Additional options include atmospheric and counterpressure filling, various machine capacities (under 15 CPM up to 60 CPM), and integration with handle applicators and rotary infeed tables.

## Featured Products

American Canning Machines offers a variety of canning solutions:

1. **AT-1 Filler/Seamer:** Entry-level, professional canning machine for smaller operations.
2. **AT-3 Filler/Seamer:** Mid-tier canning system, higher output than the AT-1.
3. **AT-6 Filler/Seamer:** Advanced model, up to 60 cans per minute with dual-lane setup; compact and mobile, easy maintenance.
4. **CP-4 Filler/Seamer:** Efficient can filling and seaming for various production sizes.
5. **PTA Can Handle Applicator:** Automates handle application for multipack cans.
6. **RT-212 Infeed Table:** Optimizes product flow into the canning line, supporting speed and consistency.

Production capacities range from less than 15 CPM to over 30 CPM, with flexible filling types for a range of craft beverage producers.

## Marketing Focus

American Canning Machines positions itself as the go-to provider of compact, affordable, easy-to-use, and mobile canning equipment for small and medium batch production. The equipment is **Made in the USA** and targets craft beverage producers—such as craft beer, cider, seltzer, and other small-batch beverage makers—seeking professional results at an entry-level price point. Key marketing messages include speed, scalability, and simplicity of maintenance and operation, supporting packaging operations without compromising quality or space.

**Target audience:** Craft beverage companies, small to medium beverage producers, and entrepreneurs seeking accessible, mobile, and scalable canning solutions.
