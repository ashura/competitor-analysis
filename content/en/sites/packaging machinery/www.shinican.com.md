## Basic Information

SHIN I MACHINERY WORKS CO., LTD., established in 1956 in Taichung, Taiwan, specializes in the manufacture of can making machinery, food canning machines, and twist-off cap making equipment. The company started with punching dies and semi-automatic machines before developing advanced automatic can making lines. With a factory located in Chingshui, Taichung, and currently over 50 employees, SHIN I MACHINERY WORKS CO., LTD. has expanded its operations and exports products to more than 70 countries worldwide. The company emphasizes innovation and quality, providing solutions for both domestic and international clients in the packaging and canning industries.
