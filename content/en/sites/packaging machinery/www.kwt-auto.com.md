## Basic Information

KWT Machine Systems Co., Ltd. is a Taiwanese company established in 1997 and is recognized as a leading manufacturer of packaging machinery. The company offers comprehensive solutions including filling, capping, and labeling machines, serving various industries such as chemical, household, and food sectors. Its headquarters is located in Taiping District, Taichung City, Taiwan, and it is known for its advanced technology and strong R&D capability. KWT positions itself as a partner for quality design and manufacturing internationally.

## Featured Products

KWT Machine Systems Co., Ltd. specializes in manufacturing primary packaging machinery, offering a diverse range of solutions for industries such as chemical, household, food & beverage, and pharmaceutical. The company's featured products include:

1. **Bottle Unscrambler** – Automates bottle orientation and feeding for production lines.
2. **Robotic Bottle Unscrambler** – Advanced automation for high-speed bottle handling.
3. **Rinsing Machine** – Cleans bottles before filling.
4. **Filling Machine** – Includes various types such as piston pump fillers, flow meter fillers, and overflow filling machines, suitable for liquids of different viscosities.
5. **Pouch Filling & Capping Machine** – For pre-made spouted pouches.
6. **Capping Machine** – Automates cap application for containers, including trigger/pump cappers and side-wrap cappers.
7. **Labeling Machine** – Solutions for top, side, and wrap labeling.
8. **Leak Tester** – Tests bottle integrity and leak resistance.
9. **Induction Sealer** – Provides water-cooled induction sealing for containers.

**Featured machine examples:**
- Induction Sealing Machine KWT-3000i: Efficient water-cooled induction sealing system.
- Semi-Auto Side-Wrap Capper KWT-130ST: Compact, efficient capping for a variety of containers.
- Inline Bottle Rinser KWT-8W series: Automatic rinsing for high throughput.
- Piston Pump Filling Machine (PS series): Precise filling for liquids with varying viscosity.
- Top Labeling Machine KWT-210: Automates label application to the top of products.
- Auto Capping Machine KWT-151AL: Designed for trigger caps, suitable for household and chemical products.

Each solution is developed for integration into full packaging lines, with options for customization based on client needs.
