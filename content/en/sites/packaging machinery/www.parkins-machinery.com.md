## Basic Information

Parkins Plastic Machinery Co., Ltd. was established in 1976 under the leadership of President C.I. Lin. The company is headquartered in Taiwan and is recognized as a major manufacturer of plastic bag-making, sealing, and cutting machines. Over 40 years, Parkins has developed a comprehensive product range and has become a market leader in the plastic machinery industry in Taiwan. They emphasize quality, stability, and innovation in their manufacturing process, and the Parkins brand is known for its commitment to excellence. Their products are exported to over 60 countries globally, serving clients in various international markets.
