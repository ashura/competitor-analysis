## Basic Information

AV Access, founded in 2015 and based in Zhejiang, China, is a professional developer and manufacturer of audio-visual products. The company specializes in Pro AV and IP AV solutions, offering an extensive lineup of HDMI and KVM extenders, AV over IP solutions, matrices, and switches. Their products serve sectors including education, government, hospitality, and home entertainment, with a strong emphasis on easy installation and high reliability. AV Access is recognized for their robust R&D team and innovation capacity in the Pro AV industry.

## Key Features

AV Access specializes in the development and manufacturing of professional Pro AV and IP AV equipment. Its key features and main capabilities include:

- A broad portfolio of AV products such as HDMI/HDBaseT extenders, KVM extenders, KVM switch docking stations, AV over IP encoders and decoders, HDMI matrices, and USB peripherals, suitable for both business and home use.
- Multiple AV over IP product lines built on different platforms, including H.264/H.265, JPEG2000, and SDVoE, enabling scalable and high-quality AV distribution over networks.
- Plug-and-play designs and high reliability, making setup and operation easy for clients worldwide.
- Innovative features like matrix switching, video wall support, fast seamless switching, visual control via the VDirector App, and remote management.
- Support for high video resolutions up to 10K@120Hz in some KVM switches and dual 4K UHD video transmission on docking stations.
- Focus on providing cost-effective, high-quality products that have been well received globally.

## Marketing Focus

AV Access focuses on providing high-quality, plug-and-play, and cost-effective Pro AV (audio/visual) products and smart home office devices. Their marketing emphasizes reliability, innovation, and affordability, with a strong R&D background and modern production capacity. Core values highlighted are quality-first manufacturing, customer-oriented service, and budget-friendly solutions. Their target audience includes professional AV integrators, installers, and businesses seeking AV extension, switching, and splitting solutions for environments such as hotels, corporate offices, universities, and large organizations—evidenced by partnerships with clients like Hilton, Rolls-Royce, Puma, SECO, and the University of Toronto. They also offer OEM/ODM services for businesses wanting custom AV solutions.

## Technical Strength

AV Access demonstrates significant technical strength and competitiveness as a global provider of professional audio/video (Pro AV) products and solutions. Key technical advantages include:

- **Strong R&D Capability**: Over 35% of the company's staff are R&D engineers, forming one of the most innovative teams with the largest number of R&D employees in the professional A/V field. This underpins the company’s product innovation and quality.
- **Modern Manufacturing**: AV Access operates a modern factory with a 10,000 square meter production area and advanced processes, including SMT, soldering, firmware uploading, and assembly. Annual production capacities reach 720,000 for simple Pro-AV products, 230,000 for IP-related products, and 30,000 for matrix products.
- **Product Portfolio and Technology**: Specializes in plug-and-play, cost-effective Pro AV solutions, including AV over IP products utilizing H.264/H.265, JPEG2000, and SDVoE platforms. These technologies enable high-quality AV extension, switching, and splitting across sectors like smart home, education, corporate, retail, government, and entertainment.
- **Industry Alliances and Standards**: The company is a contributing member of the HDBaseT alliance, illustrating its role in industry standards and access to the latest transmission technologies.
- **Global Business Presence**: AV Access has a worldwide sales and service network, with more than 5,000 partners in over 50 countries and regions, and long-term collaboration with over 30,000 installers.
- **Reputation and Client Base**: Maintains a positive rating above 99% and is trusted by major international clients, including Hilton, Rolls-Royce, Puma, SECO, and leading universities.

These factors collectively position AV Access as a technically strong and competitive player in the professional AV industry.
