## Basic Information

Highgrade Tech. Co., Ltd. was established in 2006 after spinning off from its parent group's Wall Mounting Accessory Business Unit. The company quickly captured over 60% of Taiwan's market for wall mounting accessories, with major clients including Sharp, Panasonic, LG, ChiMei, BenQ, and ViewSonic. Based in Taiwan, Highgrade Tech provides a wide range of high-quality products such as LCD wall mounts, projector mounts, monitor stands, cable management columns, mobile computer carts, and medical instrument carts. The company positions itself as a key solution provider focusing on healthcare, ergonomics, and IT infrastructure for medical environments.

## Featured Products

Highgrade Tech. Co., Ltd. specializes in innovative mounting and workstation solutions for medical, healthcare, and IT environments. Their main product categories and representative feature products include:

1. **Carts:**
   - Healthcare Mobile Computer Workstation (CNN02): Hospital computer cart designed for laptops.
   - Healthcare Trolley Workstation (CNH02): Mobile cart for displays and healthcare equipment.
   - Simple Mobile Computing Carts (CSHL2L, CSN020): Ergonomic, mobile workstations tailored for healthcare applications.

2. **Workstations:**
   - Sit-Stand Wall Mount Workstation Combo System (ORW10): Wall-mounted, height-adjustable workspace.
   - Sit-Stand Spring Arm Desk Mount Workstation (OEW12E, WST10): Flexible and ergonomic desktop workstations.
   - Thin Client Sit-Stand Combo Spring Arm Wall Mount (W8812A): Integrated arm for compact, versatile computing needs.

3. **Monitor Arms:**
   - Cantilever Spring Arm Ergonomic Adjustable Monitor Stand (ATC20): 24-inch adjustable, ergonomic monitor solution.
   - Ergonomic Spring Desk Monitor Stand (ATI40): Spring-loaded for easy adjustment.
   - Spring Cantilever Adjustable Monitor Wall Mount (ATW10): 27-inch ergonomic wall mount.

4. **Medical Arms:**
   - ALW220, ALW210 (Wall-mounted Cantilever Arms): Designed for hospital bedside infotainment.
   - ALC240 (Clamp Mounted), ALR200 (Ceiling Mounted): Configurations for flexible patient or staff use.

5. **Tablet Arms / Tablet Stands:**
   - IPA20: Universal tablet stand for major brands like Samsung and iPad.
   - IPA000: 9"–11" pad stand with key lock security.
   - IPB010: Universal tablet stand for multiple use cases.
   - IAC100: Key lock series with clamp base for secure placement.

6. **Desk Mounts:**
   - TC746A: Hex LCD monitor desk mount for multi-screen setups.
   - TS742E: Ultra slim dual LCD/LED monitor stand.
   - HA770L: Height adjustable stand for ergonomic positioning.
   - S2710B: Industrial PC stand.

7. **Projector Mounts:**
   - PB053B: Short throw projector mount (0.3–0.35 ratio).
   - PCA600, PCA530: Wall mounts specifically for short-throw projectors.
   - PL32LR, PL380: Heavy-duty ceiling mounts, ultra-tilt angle support.

8. **Wall Mounts and Accessories:**
   - Articulating/LCD/LED TV Wall Mount Brackets (e.g., ARE464, AE444, A6041, AE211, AE222, AE244A).
   - Plasma TV Ceiling Mounts (D9250 series).
