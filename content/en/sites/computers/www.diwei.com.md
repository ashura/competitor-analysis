## Basic Information
Diwei Industrial Co., Ltd. was established in 1991 and is headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan. The company is family-owned and focuses on designing, manufacturing, and exporting ergonomic products such as monitor supporting arms, TV stands, TV/monitor mounts, medical carts, and other office accessories to global markets. It operates a 4,300-square-meter factory with its own metal pressing, plastic injection, assembly, and packing plants. Diwei emphasizes quality manufacturing in Taiwan and is recognized as a reliable supplier in the screen mount and ergonomic office solutions industry.

## Key Features
Diwei Industrial Co., Ltd. is a leading manufacturer of ergonomic computer and office products established in 1991. The company specializes in developing and producing solutions that enhance comfort and efficiency in modern workplaces. Key features and capabilities include:

1. **Comprehensive Ergonomic Product Range:** Diwei offers medical monitor arms, mobile medical trolleys, mobile workstation carts, motorized PC/LCD/TV stands, projector mountings, LCD pole mounts, TV/LED monitor mounts, medicine trolleys, and various mounting systems, supporting both medical and office environments.
2. **User-Centric and Adjustable Designs:** Products incorporate adjustable elements, such as locking wheels, slide-out keyboard trays, height-adjustable workspaces, high-end flexible monitor arms, and motorized height adjustments for monitors/TVs, allowing personalized comfort and promoting healthier postures.
3. **Robust Manufacturing and Quality Control:** Diwei owns metal pressing and plastic injection facilities, along with dedicated assembly and packing plants. The company maintains ISO-9001 certification, implementing stringent quality control processes from materials to packaging.
4. **Advanced R&D and Innovation:** Diwei maintains a strong R&D team to keep up with industry trends and user feedback, continuously improving product features and introducing new solutions. Examples include the use of double pneumatic spring systems in monitor arms for flexible adjustment.
5. **Efficient Workspace Organization:** Their products are designed for optimal space utilization and efficient organization, offering integrated storage and mobile workstation solutions for improved workflow and productivity.
