## Basic Information
Founded in 1977, Yi Chun Textile Ltd. manufactures approximately five million meters of nylon, polyester, and synthetic leather fabrics annually, mainly for use in bags, luggage, umbrellas, and garments. The company is headquartered at 2F, No. 578, Sec. 1, Zhonghua Rd., Wuqi Dist., Taichung City 43549, Taiwan. Yi Chun Textile exports products to the USA, Europe, South Africa, and the Middle East, and is noted for its vertical integration with its own weaving, dyeing, PVC backing, and PU coating factories. New product lines include dual-color, shiny metallic, stretchable, and lightweight textiles.

## Key Features
Yi Chun Textile Ltd., established in 1977, is a leading textile manufacturer based in Taiwan specializing in the production of nylon, polyester, and synthetic leather fabrics. The company manufactures approximately five million meters of fabric annually, used primarily for luggage, bags, shoes, umbrellas, garments, and sporting goods. Yi Chun Textile Ltd. is known for its innovative textiles, including dual-color, shiny metallic, stretchable, and lightweight fabrics, which offer a wide range of colors and surface treatments. The company boasts vertical integration with its own textile, dyeing, and coating plants, enabling meticulous quality control and efficient production. Yi Chun Textile Ltd. serves clients worldwide, exporting to the USA, Europe, South Africa, and the Middle East.

Their strategic emphasis is on eco-friendly innovations, such as recycled and bio-based fabrics, low-impact production, and transparent life cycle assessments to support brands with measurable ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) progress. Technical performance is another key message—highlighting durable, multi-functional fabrics with properties like moisture management, stretch, UV resistance, and antibacterial effects without harmful chemicals. Yi Chun Textile Ltd. demonstrates a focus on ESG investment, especially in environmental sustainability.
