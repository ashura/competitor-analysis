## Basic Information
HSIA CHENG WOVEN TEXTILE CO., LTD., founded in 1967 in Taiwan, is a three-generation family-owned business with a long history and expertise in producing a wide range of agricultural nets and agrotextile products. The company provides solutions such as insect nets, shade nets, anti-hail nets, olive nets, and more to protect and nurture crops, advancing sustainable agriculture. Their facilities are located in both Taiwan and Vietnam, enabling efficient production and global supply. HSIA CHENG emphasizes innovation, product durability, eco-friendliness, and customization to meet diverse client needs. They have a global reach and serve prestigious agricultural companies worldwide, aiming to enhance efficiency and success for farmers and agricultural industries.

## Key Features
HSIA CHENG WOVEN TEXTILE CO., LTD. specializes in the manufacturing and exportation of high-quality agrotextile products designed primarily for agricultural applications. Established in 1967, the company has built a reputation for durability, moisture resistance, and eco-friendliness in its woven and knitted textile solutions. Key products include anti-insect nets, ground cover, shade nets (both woven and knitted), anti-hail nets, olive nets, silo nets and bags, scaffolding nets, quad/hexa nets, fencing nets, and bird nets. HSIA CHENG offers custom-made products and operates production facilities in both Taiwan and Vietnam, ensuring efficient service and quality assurance for a global clientele. The company attributes its strengths to advanced facilities, extensive experience, and a customer-centric approach, resulting in tailored solutions and long-term partnerships.
