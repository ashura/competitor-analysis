## Basic Information
DING YAO FABRIC CO., LTD., founded in 1988 in Taiwan, supplies a wide range of woven fabrics including nylon and polyester. Their monthly production capacities are 2 million meters in Taiwan and 6 million meters in China (since 2000). Products are tailored for outdoor, luggage, furniture, apparel, and footwear industries. The company is committed to customer satisfaction, continual process improvement, and sustainable development through the use of recyclable and eco-friendly materials. DING YAO FABRIC CO., LTD. has operational locations in Taipei, Taichung, Shanghai, and Guangzhou.
