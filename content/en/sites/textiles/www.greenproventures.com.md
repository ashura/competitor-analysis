## Basic Information
GreenPro Ventures Pvt Ltd. is an ISO-certified manufacturer based in Karnataka, India, operating under the brand identity of Krishi Geopack Pvt. Ltd., and is a subsidiary of Rishi FIBC Solutions Pvt. Ltd. and KT Exports (India) Pvt. Ltd. The company specializes in agricultural films, agrotextiles, and technical fabrics for protected cultivation, aquaculture, grain storage, and construction. Their large manufacturing facilities utilize advanced European technology, including 7-layer blown film plants and German non-woven fabric lines, enabling them to deliver highly customizable products. GreenPro Ventures serves a global market and aims to be the supplier of choice for innovative solutions enhancing agricultural productivity and sustainability.
