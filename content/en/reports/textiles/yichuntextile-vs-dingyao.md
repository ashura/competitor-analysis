## Basic Information

**Yi Chun Textile Ltd.**
- Founded in 1977, Taiwan.
- Annual output: ~5 million meters (nylon, polyester, synthetic leather).
- Vertically integrated (weaving, dyeing, PVC backing, PU coating).
- Exports worldwide: USA, Europe, South Africa, Middle East.
- Headquarters: Taichung City, Taiwan.
  - Sources:
    [Company Listing](https://www.company-listing.org/yi_chun_textile_ltd.html),
    [FindCompany](https://www.findcompany.com.tw/index.php/en/YI%20CHUN%20TEXTILE%20LTD.),
    [Goodsourcing](http://www.goodsourcing.com/main/exec/more/mod/corp/uid/d7af3616c1f98s23)

**DING YAO FABRIC CO., LTD.**
- Established 1988, Taiwan.
- Monthly capacity: 2 million meters (Taiwan), 6 million meters (China).
- Focus: woven nylon and polyester for outdoor/luggage/furniture/apparel/footwear.
- Locations: Taipei, Taichung (Taiwan); Shanghai & Guangzhou (China).
  - Source: [Official site](https://www.dingyao.com.tw)

## Marketing Focus

**Yi Chun Textile Ltd.**
- Emphasizes sustainable and high-performance textile solutions.
- Promotes eco-friendly innovations—recycled/bio-based fabrics; low-impact production; ESG support for brands.
- Highlights technical properties: moisture management, UV resistance, antibacterial effects without harmful chemicals.
  - Target sectors: outdoor/leisure/luggage/apparel/tents/military/medical.

  - Source: [Eco-friendly message](https://www.yichuntextile.com/msg/msg111.html)

**DING YAO FABRIC CO., LTD.**
- Focuses on professional supply of nylon/polyester/non-woven fabrics for bags/furniture/raincoats/shoes.
- Stresses sustainability via recyclable materials and multifunctional finishing.
- Targets manufacturers and brands needing specialized fabric solutions with responsive service.

  - Source: [Company overview](https://www.dingyao.com.tw)
