## Basic Information

**HSIA CHENG**
- Founded in 1967, Taiwan; family-owned, three generations.
- Manufactures a wide range of agricultural nets and agrotextiles.
- Facilities in Taiwan and Vietnam for global supply.
- Focus on innovation, eco-friendliness, durability, and customization.
- Serves global agricultural markets.
Source: [HSIA CHENG Official Website](https://www.hsiachen.com)

**GreenPro Ventures**
- Based in Karnataka, India; ISO-certified.
- Operates under Krishi Geopack Pvt. Ltd.; part of Rishi FIBC Solutions and KT Exports.
- Manufactures agricultural films, agrotextiles, technical fabrics for protected cultivation, aquaculture, storage, and construction.
- Uses advanced European technology (e.g., 7-layer blown film plants).
- Targets global market with customizable solutions.
Source: [GreenPro Ventures Official Website](https://www.greenproventures.com)
