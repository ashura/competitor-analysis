## Basic Information

**Diwei Industrial Co., Ltd.**
- Founded in 1991, headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan.
- Family-owned; specializes in ergonomic office and medical mounting products.
- Operates a 4,300 m² factory with metal pressing, plastic injection, assembly, and packing.
- Focuses on global export and quality manufacturing in Taiwan.
- [Source: Metoree](https://us.metoree.com/companies/149216/), [manufacturers.com.tw](https://www.manufacturers.com.tw/showroom-5584-4-1-0-0.php), [Alibaba](https://diwei-co.en.alibaba.com/), [SignalHire](https://www.signalhire.com/companies/diwei-industrial-co-ltd)

**Highgrade Tech. Co., Ltd.**
- Established in 2006 after a spin-off from its parent group’s Wall Mounting Accessory Business Unit.
- Quickly captured over 60% of Taiwan’s wall mounting accessory market.
- Major clients: Sharp, Panasonic, LG, ChiMei, BenQ, ViewSonic.
- Based in Taiwan; offers LCD wall mounts, monitor stands, medical carts, and more for healthcare IT and commercial markets.
- [Source: Highgrade About Us](https://www.highgrade.com.tw/en/2-2321/a/About-Us.html), [Asianavigator](https://asianavigator.com/company/1328-highgrade-tech-co-ltd), [Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/highgrade-tech-co)

## Key Features

**Diwei Industrial Co., Ltd.**
1. Wide range of ergonomic products for office and medical use (monitor arms, TV stands, medical carts).
2. Adjustable designs: locking wheels, slide-out trays, motorized adjustments for monitors/TVs.
3. Owns full manufacturing facilities—metal pressing to assembly—ensuring quality control (ISO 9001 certified).
4. Strong R&D focus; innovative features like double pneumatic spring systems.
5. Efficient workspace organization—integrated storage and mobile solutions.
   - [Market Prospects Article](https://www.market-prospects.com/articles/diwei)

**Highgrade Tech. Co., Ltd.**
1. Diverse product range: medical/commercial carts, monitor arms, wall mounts, tablet stands.
2. Healthcare & commercial focus—solutions to improve clinical workflows and ergonomics.
3. Patented innovations; strong legacy in hinge technology for adjustability/accessibility.
4. High quality with customization options; strong global presence via international exhibitions.
5. Mission-driven to enhance living/work environments through ergonomic design.

## Marketing Focus

**Diwei Industrial Co., Ltd.:**
Focuses on ergonomic mounting solutions for offices and medical environments—emphasizing high-quality Taiwanese manufacturing and continuous R&D to meet global trends in comfort and productivity across B2B/B2C sectors.

**Highgrade Tech. Co., Ltd.:**
Markets as a solution provider for healthcare IT mounting systems—targeting hospitals and clinics needing reliable ergonomic hardware; highlights patented designs and efficient supply chains for B2B healthcare system integrators.

## Technical Strength

**Diwei Industrial Co., Ltd.:**
Owns a large ISO-certified factory with end-to-end manufacturing capabilities; robust R&D team drives innovation in ergonomic solutions; offers OEM/ODM services globally; emphasizes after-sales service and long-term partnerships.

**Highgrade Tech. Co., Ltd.:**
Specializes in hinge technology and patented ergonomic designs; supplies a wide range of healthcare IT mounting solutions including motorized carts and sit–stand workstations; strong focus on product diversification and customization; recognized for technical expertise in medical display integration.
