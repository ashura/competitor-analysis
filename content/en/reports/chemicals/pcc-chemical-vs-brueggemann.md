## Basic Information

**Pan-Continental Chemical**
- Founded in 1979, headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan (registered in Taipei).
- Specializes in inorganic chemical compounds: zinc oxide, active/nano zinc oxide, zinc carbonate, copper oxide, copper carbonate, and zinc chloride.
- Operates six manufacturing plants across Taiwan, China, and Vietnam; serves global markets.
- Registered capital: NT$456 million.
  - [Source](https://www.pcc-chemical.com/), [Metoree](https://us.metoree.com/companies/191186/)

**Brueggemann**
- Established in 1868, headquartered in Heilbronn, Germany.
- Provides custom solutions in polymer additives, reducing agents, zinc compounds, specialty chemicals, and alcohols.
- Family-owned with a global reach; serves industries including pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, food, and construction.
  - [Source](https://www.brueggemann.com/en/history)

## Featured Products

**Pan-Continental Chemical**
- Zinc-based: zinc oxide (active/nano/sustainable CS AZO), zinc carbonate, zinc chloride
- Copper-based: copper oxide, copper carbonate
  - Used in rubber/EVA vulcanization, catalysts, personal care/cosmetics, plastics, PCB/semiconductors/textiles
  - [Source](https://smp.pcc-chemical.com/en/)

**Brueggemann**
Key product lines:
1. **Heat Stabilizers:** BRUGGOLEN® series for polyamides/polyolefins; classic to modern halogen-free options.
2. **Processing Agents:** Nucleating agents (P22/P252), flow improvers (P1507), lubricants.
3. **Modifiers:** Chain extenders/breakers for recycling/upcycling polymers.
4. **Impact Modifiers:** Compoline® CO/PA series for engineering plastics.
5. **Light Stabilizers:** For UV/color stability in thermoplastics.
6. **Recycling Additives:** Proprietary “repair technology” for upcycling polyolefin waste streams.

Available as powders/granules/masterbatches; customized “One-Pack” solutions for various processing needs.
  - [Source](https://www.brueggemann.com/en/polymer-additives)
