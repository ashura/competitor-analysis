## Key Features

**Parkins**
- Over 40 years of expertise in plastic bag machinery manufacturing.
- Wide range of machines (bottom sealing, T-shirt bag, perforating bag on roll, side sealing, glove machines).
- Focus on recyclable and biodegradable materials.
- Emphasizes low-carbon manufacturing.
- Offers customization and automation to boost efficiency.
[Source](https://www.parkins-machinery.com/en)

**COSMO**
- 20+ years of experience; >3,000 machines exported globally.
- ISO 9001 & CE certified.
- Advanced servo-driven systems with PLC controls.
- Diverse product range (T-shirt bag on roll, bottom/side sealing, air cushion bags).
- Known for stable quality at reasonable prices and excellent after-sales support.
[Source](https://www.prm-taiwan.com/enews/issue-255/innovative-features-cosmo039s-plastic-bag-making-machines_1705)

## Technical Strength

**Parkins**
- Four decades of technical leadership; exports to over 60 countries.
- High-performance servo systems for precision sealing/cutting.
- Supports LDPE, HDPE, PP, recycled & eco-friendly materials (e.g., NuPlastiQ®).
- Modular design; multilingual touchscreen interfaces; remote diagnostics.
[Source](https://www.market-prospects.com/articles/parkins-plastic-machinery-co-ltd-a-global-leader-in-bag-making-machine-innovation)

**COSMO**
1. Customizable modular machines for various bag types/sizes.
2. Flagship fully automatic coreless garbage bags-on-roll machine with dual feeding & auto-labeling.
3. Real-time process control reduces downtime/waste; aligns with ESG principles.
4. Decades of international experience; strong global support network.
5. Localized agent/service training ensures worldwide reliability.
[Source](https://www.prm-taiwan.com/blog/bagging-global-market-how-cosmo-machinery-earns-international-trust-through-technology-service_563)
