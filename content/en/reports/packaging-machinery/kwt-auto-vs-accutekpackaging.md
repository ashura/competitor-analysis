- Founded: 1997
- Headquarters: Taiping District, Taichung City, Taiwan
- Industry Focus: Chemical, household, food & beverage, pharmaceutical
- Positioning: Largest packaging machinery manufacturer in Taiwan; strong R&D and international presence
- Source: [KWT Official Website](https://www.kwt-auto.com/), [LinkedIn](https://tw.linkedin.com/company/kwt-machine-systems-co-ltd), [企查查](https://www.qcc.com/firm/t9227a72b5955f9f832127483a04d4d9.html)

**Accutek**
- Founded: 1989 by Edward Chocholek and sons
- Headquarters: Vista, California, USA (with locations in Texas and Florida)
- Industry Focus: Food & beverage, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, chemicals
- Positioning: One of the largest privately owned packaging machinery manufacturers in the US; turnkey solutions provider
- Source: [Accutek About](https://www.accutekpackaging.com/about/), [Packaging-Labelling Profile](https://www.packaging-labelling.com/suppliers/accutek-packaging-equipment)
