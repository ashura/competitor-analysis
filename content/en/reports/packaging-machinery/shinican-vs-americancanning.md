## Basic Information

**SHIN I MACHINERY WORKS CO., LTD.**
- Founded in 1956 in Taichung, Taiwan.
- Specializes in can making machinery, food canning machines, and twist-off cap equipment.
- Factory in Chingshui, Taichung; over 50 employees (some sources note over 300).
- Exports to more than 70 countries worldwide.
- Emphasizes innovation and quality for global packaging/canning industries.
- Sources: [Company Profile](https://www.shinican.com/profile), [Company History](https://www.shinican.com/history), [CENS.com Listing](https://www.cens.com/cens/html/en/supplier/supplier_home_4930.html), [METPACK Exhibitor List](https://www.metpack.de/market-leader/exhibitor-list/detail/shin-i-machinery-works-co-ltd-c032c)

**American Canning Machines**
- Based in Austin, Texas, USA.
- Manufactures small-format beverage packaging equipment for craft beverage makers.
- Focus on compact, reliable, easy-to-use canning systems for small/medium batch production.
- Employs 5–9 people; estimated annual revenue between $500K and $1M.
- Supports beverage companies with packaging supplies/services.
- Sources: [datanyze.com](https://www.datanyze.com/companies/american-canning-machines/547256521), [craftypint.com](https://craftypint.com/business/1465/american-canning-machines), [opencorporates.com](https://opencorporates.com/companies/us_tx/0802806702), [cbinsights.com](https://www.cbinsights.com/company/american-canning)
