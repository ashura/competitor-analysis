## Basic Information

**Kun Huang**
- Founded in Taiwan; over 60 years of experience.
- Specializes in polyurethane (PU) foam materials for sports, military, footwear, electronics, and more.
- Strong focus on eco-friendly processes and international environmental compliance.
- Comprehensive services: R&D, design, prototyping, quality control, and mass production.
- Source: [Company Profile](https://www.kun-huang.com/en/abouts/company-profile)

**Arneplant**
- Founded in 1999 in Arnedo, La Rioja, Spain.
- Family-owned; specializes in insoles, foams, and microfibers for footwear.
- Focuses on innovation, breathability, comfort, and sustainability.
- Production facilities in Spain and Vietnam; ~80 employees.
- Source: [About Us](https://arneplant.com/en/about-us/), [Arneplant Group](https://arneplant.com/en/arneplant-group/)

## Key Features

**Kun Huang**
1. Wide range of PU foam products (open cell foam, high density sheets, custom formulas).
2. Environmental sustainability embedded in manufacturing.
3. Diverse market applications across multiple industries.
4. Robust R&D with proprietary foaming technology and customization capabilities.
5. International recognition through trade shows and global brand partnerships.

**Arneplant**
1. Specializes in removable insoles for all footwear types—focus on comfort and health.
2. Manufactures high-density open-cell foams for cushioning/breathability.
3. Produces industrial fabrics tailored to the footwear sector.
4. Develops highly absorbent microfibers with a soft texture for footwear interiors.
5. Emphasizes technological innovation and sustainability with global reach.
