## Basic Information

**Aok Valve Stem Seals Ltd.**
- Founded in 1995, Nantou City, Nantou County, Taiwan.
- Specializes in automotive rubber oil seals, valve stem seals, bonded seals, O-rings, bushings, absorbers, and custom rubber-metal parts.
- Provides OEM/ODM services with a strong focus on product quality and R&D.
- Serves global automotive and industrial markets.
- [Source](https://www.aok-oilseal.com/msg/about.html)

**NIYOK SEALING PARTS CO., LTD.**
- Over 40 years of experience; based in Lukang Township, Changhua, Taiwan.
- Manufactures seals and oil seal products using advanced 3D CAD and modern facilities.
- Designs and produces high-quality rubber/sealing components for automotive and industrial sectors worldwide.
- [Source](https://www.niyok.com.tw/en/page/company-profile.html)
