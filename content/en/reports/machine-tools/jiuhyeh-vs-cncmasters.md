## Basic Information

**CNC Masters**
- Founded in 1990, located in California, USA.
- Specializes in CNC milling machines, lathes, benchtop mills, and automation equipment.
- Serves both industrial and hobbyist markets.
- Emphasizes U.S.-built machines and customer support.
- [Source](https://www.cncmasters.com/about-cnc-masters/)

**Jiuh-Yeh Precision**
- Based in Taichung City, Taiwan.
- Manufacturer of milling machines and machining centers under the Millstar brand.
- Product range includes vertical/horizontal machining centers, bed type and knee type milling machines (CNC/conventional).
- Focuses on global markets (Asia, Americas, Europe) with an emphasis on innovation and customization.
- [Source](https://www.jiuhyeh.com)

## Key Features

**CNC Masters**
- Conversational programming for easy program tweaks
- Vertical Machining Centers for tough materials
- Suitable for production/prototyping; woodworking & engraving
- Options: quick tool changers, coolant kits, R8 collets
- Adjustable jog feed rate & variable spindle speed
- User-friendly G Code/M Code software
- Compact footprint; ideal for small shops/hobbyists
- Conversion kits for manual-to-CNC upgrades
- Robust U.S.-based support
- [Source](https://www.cncmasters.com/)

**Jiuh-Yeh Precision**
- Wide range: vertical/horizontal machining centers, bed/knee type mills (CNC/conventional)
- Robust design using finite element analysis & high-strength cast iron columns
- Precision-ground tables & hardened slide surfaces
- Integrated automatic lubrication & cooling systems
- High-speed precision ball screws; AC servo-driven axes
- Roller-type linear guideways for rigidity & precision
- Modular technology and custom solutions available
- Emphasis on stability, safety (limit switches), durability, and lifelong service
- [Source](https://www.jiuhyeh.com)
