## Basic Information

**Ocean Technologies**
- Taiwan-based manufacturer specializing in CNC electric discharge machines (EDMs).
- Offers CNC fast hole drilling, micro hole EDM, and super drill EDM machines.
- Serves industries such as aerospace, wind power, and precision microscopy.
- Headquarters: Taichung City, Taiwan.
- Focus on technological innovation and high-quality manufacturing.
  - [Source](https://www.octece.com)

**Sparkonix**
- Indian pioneer in Electric Discharge Machines (EDMs), including micro-controller/ZNC EDMs, CNC EDM Drills, and Tap Removal Machines.
- Established in the 1960s; first to introduce Metal Arc Disintegrators in India.
- Over 7,500 installations worldwide; serves 30+ industries with 500+ applications.
- Headquarters: Pune, India.
  - [Source](https://www.sparkonix.com)

## Key Features

**Ocean Technologies**
- Specializes in high-precision CNC EDMs for fast/micro hole drilling.
- Product lines include River series and OCT series for various industrial needs.
- Advanced digital control systems, high drilling speed, minimal electrode wear.
- Virtual showrooms and VR factory tours available for clients.
  - [Source](https://www.octece.com)

**Sparkonix**
- Manufactures India's highest-selling EDM Spark Erosion Machines.
- Wide range: CNC EDMs, EDM Drills, Tap Removal Machines, Pulse Generators, Tyre Mould EDMs, Twin Head EDMs.
- Focus on faster machining cycles and high-quality finish across diverse applications.
- Strong after-sales support and dealer network across India’s major cities.
  - [Source](https://www.sparkonix.com)
