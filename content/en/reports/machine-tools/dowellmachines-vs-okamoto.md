- Industries Served: Broad manufacturing sectors globally
- In-house Manufacturing: Controls all production stages from casting to assembly

## Technical Strength

**Dowell**
- Nearly three decades of technical refinement in high precision grinding machines.
- Expertise in advanced materials (ceramics/quartz/silicon wafers).
- Professional R&D team with a user-centered design philosophy.
  [Source](https://www.dowellmachines.com/company-profile)

**Okamoto**
1. In-house pattern/casting production for rigid machine bases.
2. Over 90 years of experience evolving from gear grinders to total abrasive processing solutions.
3. Technical innovations—angular contact bearings for spindle rigidity/stability even with hard-to-cut materials.
4. Over 60,000 machines operating globally—a mark of industry leadership.
