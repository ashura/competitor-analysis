## Basic Information

**TOP-ONE**
- Founded in 1989, based in Taichung City, Taiwan.
- Employs over 100 people in Taiwan and Mainland China.
- Factories in Kunming and Shanghai; global service network.
- Specializes in CNC vertical milling machines, bed mills, wheel lathes, ID/OD grinding machines, column machines.
- Serves industries including aerospace, automotive, medical, energy, shipbuilding, mold/die, bicycle, home appliances.

  [Source](https://www.topone-m.com/company-profile)

**Atrump**
- Incorporated in 1989; based in Pomona, California, USA.
- Produces CNC/manual machine tools: bed mills, knee mills, machining centers, precision lathes, grinders.
- Focuses on industrial manufacturing and machining sectors.

  [Sources](https://atrumpparts.com/about-us/) | [Data](https://www.datanyze.com/companies/atrump-machinery/3310222)
