## Basic Information

**Chevalier (FALCON MACHINE TOOLS CO., LTD.)**
- Founded in Taiwan, specializes in high-precision grinders, lathes, and milling machines.
- Global market presence with applications in aerospace, automotive, energy, medical, semiconductor, mold, and automation.
- Focus on digital transformation and technological innovation (e.g., proprietary SMART iControl and iSurface CNC systems).
- Facilities and headquarters in Changhua, Taiwan.
- Source: [Chevalier – About Us](https://www.chevaliertw.com)

**Okamoto Machine Tool Works, Ltd.**
- Established in 1935 in Annaka, Gunma Prefecture, Japan.
- Evolved from a gear grinding machine company to a comprehensive abrasive processing machine manufacturer.
- Produces a wide range of machine tools and semiconductor processing equipment.
- Operates an integrated manufacturing system (from casting to final assembly) in-house.
- Global presence serving diverse manufacturing industries.
- Source: [Okamoto – Company Overview](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/company/)

## Key Features

**Chevalier**
- Wide range of CNC and precision machinery: surface grinders, vertical grinding centers, profile grinders, rotary surface grinders, double-sided fine grinders, double column grinders.
- Advanced milling solutions: 5-axis machining centers, turret milling machines.
- Diverse turning solutions including vertical turning lathes.
- Proprietary SMART+ technologies for automation and digital transformation.
- Custom machines for industry-specific needs (e.g., non-circular piston contouring lathes).
    - Source: [Chevalier – Products](https://www.chevaliertw.com/en/product-categories)

**Okamoto**
- Integrated manufacturing system ensures control over all production stages for high quality/reliability.
- T-shaped monocoque structure for rigidity/accuracy; ball screw drives & linear guides for precise positioning.
- Advanced grinding functions: flat automatic cycle grinding, shift plunge/traverse/multi-position grinding.
- Over 60,000 machines operating globally; strong presence in semiconductor equipment as well as general grinding machines.
    - Sources:
        - [Okamoto – Production System](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/production/)
        - [Okamoto – PSG Series Features](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/products/psg-ca3/)
        - [Okamoto – Search by Machines](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/search-by-machines/)

## Technical Strength

**Chevalier**
1. Broad product range with strong R&D investment—proprietary human-machine interfaces and automation systems (SMART iControl/iSurface/iMCS).
2. Serves diverse industries with global subsidiaries/distributors across more than 80 countries.
3. Emphasizes “Precision Manufacturing” through rigorous QC and advanced assembly/materials departments; led by experienced engineers/managers.
    - Source: [Chevalier – Our Vision](https://www.chevaliertw.com/en/abouts/our-vision)

**Okamoto**
1. In-house design & production from wooden patterns/casting to final assembly—enables highly rigid machine construction from scratch.
2. Over 90 years’ experience evolving into a total abrasive processing solution provider for both machine tools & semiconductors.
3. Technical innovations such as angular contact bearing spindles for high rigidity/stable performance—even on hard-to-cut materials; over 60k machines installed globally demonstrates leadership in precision grinding tech.
    - Sources:
        - [Okamoto – Production System](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/production/)
        - [Okamoto – OGM Series Features](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/products/ogm-ncag3/)
        - [Company Overview/Product List](https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/company/, https://www.okamoto.co.jp/en/search-by-machines/)
