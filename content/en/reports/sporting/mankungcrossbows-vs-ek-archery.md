## Basic Information

- **EK Archery**
  - Origin: Taiwan, under Poe Lang Enterprise Co., Ltd.
  - Over 30 years in the archery industry; brand launched globally in 2015.
  - Known for innovation (e.g., first semi-automatic repeating crossbow).
  - Distributed in 20+ countries, especially strong in Europe and the USA.
  - Focus on quality control and blending traditional expertise with modern tech.
  - [Source](https://www.ek-archery.com/about/story/), [Outfit4Events](https://www.outfit4events.com/eur/category/1017-brand-ek-archery/), [USPTO](https://uspto.report/TM/97921525)

- **Man Kung**
  - Headquartered in Taichung City, Taiwan.
  - Established in 1988, operates in foundries and sporting goods.
  - Recognized as one of the world’s largest archery manufacturers.
  - Focus on R&D, product quality, and international/domestic markets.
  - [Dun & Bradstreet](https://www.dnb.com/business-directory/company-profiles.man_kung_enterprise_co_ltd.21c4c699f710052cdee6c871f117c390.html), [台灣公司網](https://www.twincn.com/item.aspx?no=23101796)
