- Specialization: Design, manufacturing, R&D of tungsten carbide cutting tools (end mills, drills, reamers)

- Noted for: High-quality tools and technical expertise; innovative products and dependable service

- Founded: 1996
- Location: Wurih District, Taichung City, Taiwan
- Specialization: Design, production, and global supply of solid carbide cutting tools (end mills, ball nose end mills, corner radius end mills)
- Industries served: Aerospace, medical treatment, die/mold manufacturing; exports to 30+ countries worldwide
