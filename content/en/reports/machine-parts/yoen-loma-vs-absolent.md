## Basic Information

**Lotus Innovation Technology Co., Ltd.**
- Taiwan-based manufacturer specializing in oil mist collectors, air cleaners, and smart devices for industrial air purification.
- Products target machining processes (lathing, milling, grinding, drilling, etc.).
- Emphasizes international certifications and compliance.
- Contact: No.7, Sec. 1, Zhongshan Rd., Taiping Dist., Taichung City 411, Taiwan; TEL: +886-4-22787755.
  [Source](https://www.yoen-loma.com), [Source](https://www.twincn.com/item.aspx?no=24828579)

**Absolent AB**
- Swedish company founded in 1993; leader in industrial air filtration (oil mist, dust, smoke).
- Solutions for metalworking, die casting, cold forming, welding, etc.
- Headquartered in Gothenburg; global presence with subsidiaries in US, Germany, France, China.
  [Source](https://absolent.com/about-us/our-history/), [Source](https://us.metoree.com/companies/218939/)

## Key Features

**Lotus Innovation**
- Oil mist collectors and air cleaners for industrial settings
- Smoke/dust air cleaners and industrial HEPA purifiers
- AI digital smart devices for system monitoring and maintenance
- International certifications (SGS, TTR, CE)
- Wide applicability across machining processes
[Source](https://www.yoen-loma.com/)

**Absolent AB**
- Industrial air filtration: oil mist/smoke/dust/fume removal
- Compact and high-performance filters (machine-mounted or centralized)
- Patented systems: CleanChange™ (maintenance), True Downflow™ (airflow)
- Customized solutions; strong environmental focus
[Source](https://absolent.com/about-us/), [Source](https://absolent.com/product-group/ad-dust-fume-extraction/)

## Marketing Focus

**Lotus Innovation**
- Targets factories/machining workshops needing air purification for lathes/mills/grinders/drills.
- Emphasis on certified products and workplace safety.
- Global market orientation with international approvals.
[Source](https://www.yoen-loma.com/)

**Absolent AB**
- B2B focus on large industrial manufacturers.
- Metalworking (~45% sales), battery/EV/pharma/composites industries.
- Messaging emphasizes compliance, low total cost of ownership, customization.
- Consultative sales approach; premium service subscriptions; digital marketing to technical buyers.
[Source](https://portersfiveforce.com/blogs/target-market/absolentgroup)

## Technical Strength

**Lotus Innovation**
- Broad product range for various machining processes.
- Strong on international certification/compliance.
- Integration of AI-based smart devices for environmental monitoring/control.
[Source](https://www.yoen-loma.com)

**Absolent AB**
- Broadest technology portfolio in the sector; patented fiber materials & self-draining functions.
- Modular product architecture for customization/scalability.
- Advanced digital tools (IoT monitoring/predictive maintenance).
- In-house R&D focused on sustainability and innovation.
