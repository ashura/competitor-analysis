## Basic Information

**Luyang Technology Co., Ltd.**
- Founded: 1981
- Headquarters: Changhua County, Taiwan
- Specializes in: Motors, gear reducers, ring blowers, vacuum pumps
- Markets served: North America, Europe, Japan, India, Southeast Asia
- Applications: Machinery, automation equipment, packaging, food processing, medical devices
- Source: [Luyang About](https://www.luyangmotor.com)

**Oriental Motor U.S.A. Corp.**
- Founded: U.S. subsidiary established in 1978 (Parent company founded in 1885, Japan)
- Headquarters: Torrance, California (U.S.)
- Specializes in: AC motors, stepper motors, servo motors, actuators, drivers
- Markets served: North America (primary), with global reach via parent company
- Applications: Conveyors, linear/rotational motion systems, cooling solutions for automation
- Source: [Oriental Motor Company](https://www.orientalmotor.com/company/index.html)

- Over 50,000 standard products globally available
