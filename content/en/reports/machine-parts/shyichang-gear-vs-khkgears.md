## Basic Information

**SHYI CHANG**
- Founded in 1999, based in Taiwan.
- Specializes in precision gears and machining parts.
- Utilizes advanced gear grinding, hobbing, and shaping equipment from Switzerland, Germany, and Japan.
- Products are used in automotive, motorcycle, electric vehicles, machine tools, speed reducers, agricultural machinery, and for custom gear manufacturing.
- Focuses on high quality and accuracy.
  - [Source](https://www.shyichang-gear.com)

**KHK Gears**
- Headquartered in Saitama, Japan; over 80 years of experience.
- Maintains inventory of 30,000+ gear products for fast delivery.
- Offers both standard stock and custom gears.
- Serves industries such as robotics, machine tools, medical devices, industrial automation.
- Known for Japanese manufacturing standards and global sales network (USA, UK, India, Australia).
  - [Source](https://khkgears.net)
