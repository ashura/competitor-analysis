## Basic Information

- **TANSHING**
  - Established in 1995, headquartered in Taichung City, Taiwan.
  - Specializes in high-precision CNC rotary tables, indexing tables, and auto pallet changers.
  - Focuses on technological innovation and strict quality control, with advanced manufacturing facilities imported from the USA, Germany, Switzerland, and Japan.
  - Products are globally recognized for quality and reliability.
  - Sources: [TANSHING About Us](https://www.tanshing.com/en/about/about-us), [Market Prospects](https://www.market-prospects.com/articles/tanshing)

- **TSUDAKOMA**
  - Founded in 1909 in Kanazawa, Japan.
  - Major manufacturer of textile machinery (air jet/water jet looms), composite machinery, and precision machine tool attachments.
  - Known for pioneering automation in textiles and introducing Japan’s first automatic lay-up machine for carbon fiber materials.
  - Global presence with production/service bases across Asia, Europe, and North America.
  - Sources: [TSUDAKOMA Company History](https://www.tsudakoma.co.jp/english/company/history/index.html)

## Key Features

- **TANSHING**
    - Patented dual lead worm drive system for high torque and accuracy.
    - Broad lineup: CNC rotary tables (4th/5th axis), tilting rotary tables, hydraulic index tables, auto pallet changers.
    - Custom solutions via strong R&D; rapid development cycles.
    - In-house manufacturing of ~90% of parts for quality control.
    - Products serve automotive, aerospace, electronics, medical devices, and mold manufacturing sectors globally.
    - Sources: [TANSHING Technology](https://www.tanshing.com/en/about/technology), [Market Prospects](https://www.market-prospects.com/articles/tanshing)

- **TSUDAKOMA**
    - Leading textile machinery manufacturer (air jet/water jet looms).
    - Composite machinery for CFRP processing (fiber lay-up machines, prepreg slitters).
    - High-precision machine tool attachments: NC rotary tables, vises, manual tables.
    - Own foundry (Matto Plant) for high-quality castings.
    - Renowned for innovation and establishing industry standards in both textile and machine tool sectors.
    - Global sales/service network for comprehensive support.
    - Source: [TSUDAKOMA English Site](https://www.tsudakoma.co.jp/english/)

## Marketing Focus

- **TANSHING**
    - Emphasizes precision engineering and innovative technology (e.g., patented worm drive).
    - Targets industries needing high reliability—automotive, aerospace, electronics, medical devices.
    - Positions as a global supplier to machine tool manufacturers seeking to enhance machining efficiency and product quality.
    - Source: [Market Prospects](https://www.market-prospects.com/articles/tanshing)

    - Places customer needs at the center of its marketing mix (product/pricing/distribution/promotion).

## Technical Strength

- **TANSHING**
    - Patented dual lead worm drive technology enables matching product specs to specific machining conditions. ([SMP TANSHING Tech Overview](https://smp.tanshing.com/en/))
    - Special materials (aluminum bronze) used for durability; strict internal production controls (~90% in-house).
    - Strong international certification foundation (ISO9001).
    - Aggressive R&D investment; global competitiveness comparable to Japanese firms. ([Equipmentimes Profile](https://m.equipmentimes.com/supplier/TANSHING-ACCURATE-INDUSTRIAL-CO-LTD_307_1/about.htm))

- **TSUDAKOMA**
    - Noted for consumer-driven innovation; first-mover advantage in new product launches within capital goods sector. ([EMBA SWOT Analysis](https://embapro.com/frontpage/swotcoanalysis/16001-tsudakoma-corp))
    - Market leadership with rapid scaling of successful products; high profit margins relative to peers.
    - Extensive dealer network supports global reach; strong talent management ensures ongoing technical excellence.
