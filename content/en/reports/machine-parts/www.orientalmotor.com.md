## Basic Information

Oriental Motor U.S.A. Corp. is the U.S. subsidiary of Oriental Motor, a company that originated from Japan in 1885 making handmade motors. Their product range includes AC motors, speed controllers, position control devices, mechanical motion products, and thermal management solutions. Oriental Motor U.S.A. Corp. was established in 1978 and is headquartered at 570 Alaska Avenue, Torrance, California, with an international sales and service presence spanning North America, Europe, and Asia. The company provides solutions for conveyors, linear motion, rotational mechanisms, and cooling applications in the industrial automation sector.

## Technical Strength

Oriental Motor U.S.A. Corp. demonstrates significant technical strength in motion systems with a broad portfolio of around 50,000 products, including stepper motors, servo motors, AC and brushless DC motors, linear/rotary actuators, network communication products, and cooling fans. Their products are widely applied across medical, food packaging, semiconductor, factory automation, robotics, environmental, and automatic guided vehicle industries.

Technologically, they offer proprietary solutions like the αSTEP closed-loop stepper motor system, which combines the reliability of open-loop stepper control with the precision of closed-loop servo control for highly accurate positioning and error correction. The company emphasizes continuous innovation and quality, conducting R&D both in Japan and the United States, and was the first in Japan to standardize compact motors. Their FLEX concept allows for easy integration with industrial networks, providing scalable automation solutions. Oriental Motor also focuses on energy efficiency, rapid customization, and fast delivery, supported by a global service and technical support network.

## Environment / Society / Governance

Oriental Motor U.S.A. Corp. emphasizes environmental, social, and governance (ESG) measures focused primarily on environmental conservation. Their efforts include energy savings through the development of high-efficiency, compact motors, conservation of natural resources by promoting longer product life and reducing materials used, and strict control over chemical substances in products. The company adheres to international standards such as IEC 62474, and prioritizes 'Green Procurement' to minimize environmental impact.

They comply with multiple global regulations, including:
- Canada’s Prohibition of Certain Toxic Substances Regulations (phasing out BNST)
- EU RoHS Directive (restricting hazardous substances)
- EU REACH Regulation (chemical substance management)
- China’s Measures for Administration of Pollution Control of Electronic Information Products Act, ensuring proper labeling and control of hazardous substances

Implementation of ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 standards for quality and environmental management is also a core practice.
