## Basic Information

**Sonyung Industry**
- Founded in 1989, headquartered in Lukang Township, Changhua, Taiwan.
- Specializes in automation equipment components—ball screw support units, servo motor brackets, shaft couplings, actuators.
- Serves industries including aerospace, automotive, robotics, semiconductor machinery, CNC processing, medical equipment.
- Known for stable quality, fast delivery, and reasonable pricing.
- ISO9001 certified; products comply with ROHS standards.
- Source: [Commerce.com.tw](https://www.commerce.com.tw/modules.php?modules=company&action=company_inside&ID=C0012562)

**NSK Europe**
- European subsidiary of Tokyo-based NSK Ltd., established 1916; European HQ in Maidenhead, UK.
- Manufactures bearings, linear motion products, automotive components for industrial and automotive sectors.
- Operates multiple sites across Europe; 2,976 employees (FY2024); sales > €587 million.
- Global reputation for pioneering bearing development and advanced technology solutions.
- Source: [NSK Corporate Profile](https://www.nsk.com/eu-en/company/about-us/corporate-profile/)
