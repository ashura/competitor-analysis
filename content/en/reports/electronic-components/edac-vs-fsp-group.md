## Basic Information

**EDAC POWER ELECTRONICS CO., LTD.**
- Founded in 1998, headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
- Specializes in standard and custom power supply solutions: AC/DC switching power supplies, USB Type-C adapters, DC/DC converters, battery chargers, LED power supplies.
- Serves ICT, medical, e-bike, lighting, industrial sectors.
- Employs approximately 500 people.
- Production & R&D centers in Suzhou, China.
  - [Source](https://www.edac.com.tw/zh-tw/abouts/company-profile)

**FSP TECHNOLOGY INC.**
- Established in 1993, headquartered in Taoyuan, Taiwan.
- Manufactures electronic power supplies and components: AC/DC power supplies, battery chargers, PV inverters.
- Markets served include consumer electronics, lighting units, edge computing, medical devices.
- Provides OEM/ODM services and emphasizes green technologies.
  - [Source](https://pitchbook.com/profiles/company/165185-20), [Source](https://www.fsp-group.com/en/timeline.html)
