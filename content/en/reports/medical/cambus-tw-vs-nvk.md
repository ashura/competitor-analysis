## Basic Information

**Cambus**
- Founded in 1997, headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan.
- Specializes in engineering and manufacturing custom solutions for medical cable assemblies, connectors, wire harnesses, and plastic injection moldings.
- Offers services such as tooling, stamping, CNC machining parts, SMT processing, and PCB manufacturing.
- Focus on quality and customer-oriented engineering support in the medical device sector.
  - [Source](https://www.cambus-tw.com/)
  - [Source](https://www.allproducts.com/medical/cambus/)
  - [Source](https://www.manufacturers.com.tw/showroom-7134-3-1-0-3700.php)

**New V-Key**
- Established in 1989, based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
- Major manufacturer of medical cables, connectors, accessories, and cable assemblies.
- Focuses on OEM/ODM production for medical applications (ECG/EKG/EEG/EMG cables, nurse call cords, electrodes).
- Strong emphasis on quality management with GMP, ISO 13485, CE, and FDA registrations.

## Key Features

**Cambus**
- Over 20 years of expertise in OEM & ODM solutions for medical, electronics, automation, and military industries.
- Custom design/manufacture of medical cable assemblies and connectors (ECG/EKG/Redel/Lemo types).
- Precision injection-molded plastic components.
- One-stop outsourcing: CNC lathe parts, electronics parts sourcing.
- Strict adherence to international standards (IEC 60601-1); safety features like locking mechanisms/color-coding.
- Capable of both low/high-volume production with custom tooling and PCB assembly.

Sources:
- [Cambus Overview](https://www.cambus-tw.com/)
- [Cambus Products](https://www.cambus-tw.com/product.html)

**New V-Key**
- Decades of experience in custom/OEM/ODM medical cable assemblies and accessories.
- Comprehensive product range: ECG/EKG/EEG/EMG cables/adapters/sockets/connectors/electrodes/nurse call cords/sensors.
- Continuous improvement focus; strict quality policy; certified by multiple international standards (ISO/GMP/FDA/CE).
- Custom solutions for diverse industries including communications/military/aviation/computers.

Sources:
- [New V-Key Overview](https://www.nvk.com.tw/)
