## Basic Information

- **AN CHING INDUSTRY**
  - Founded in 1977, based in Chang Hwa City, Taiwan.
  - Specializes in manufacturing ID card accessories: badge holders, clips, reels, lanyards, and hardware.
  - Uses advanced plastic injection molding and metal pressing technologies.
  - Global client base, focused on rapid response and detailed customer service.
  - [Source](https://www.youridpartner.com)

- **Brady People ID**
  - Originated as Comprehensive Identification Products (CIPI) in 1966, Massachusetts, USA.
  - Now part of Brady Corporation (NYSE: BRC).
  - Focuses on identification, safety, and security solutions with patents in medical equipment/software.
  - Manufacturing facilities in North America and China; broad product range.
  - [Source](https://www.bradypeopleid.com/about-us/)
