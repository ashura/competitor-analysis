## Basic Information

- **Ranox Enterprise Inc.**
  - Taiwan-based manufacturer and supplier of paint spray equipment.
  - Offers automatic/manual spray guns, air agitators, pressure tanks, hoses, and accessories—all made in Taiwan.
  - Applications span industrial production, building decoration, transportation, boutique processing, and consumer goods.
  - Strong emphasis on product safety, quality assurance, reliability, and OEM services.
  - Offices in Taipei City; factory in Changhua County.
  - [Source](https://www.ranoxspray.com)

- **Anest Iwata Americas**
  - North American branch of Japan’s ANEST IWATA Corporation (nearly 100 years of history).
  - Specializes in air compressors, vacuum pumps, coating equipment, and fluid application systems.
  - Serves industrial, auto refinishing, food & beverage, pharmaceutical, woodworking, R&D sectors.
  - Core values: passion, innovation, creativity; delivers engineered solutions for diverse industrial needs.
  - [Source](https://www.anestiwataamericas.com)

## Technical Strength

- **Ranox Enterprise Inc.**
   * All products manufactured in Taiwan—emphasis on quality/safety especially for pressure tanks.
   * Product breadth covers automatic/manual spray systems with adaptability across many industries/applications including automotive refinish and boutique processing.
   * Differentiates from low-cost competitors by prioritizing reliability/security; continuous development & strong after-sales service support OEM/private label launches worldwide.

   * [Source](https://www.ranoxspray.com)

- **Anest Iwata Americas**
   * Pioneer of reciprocating compressors (1926) and rotary screw compressors (1977); first with fan-cooled oil-free scroll compressors globally (1991).
   * Major market share in oil-free compressor/vacuum pump segments in Japan; recognized technical leadership especially for green technologies (oil-free).
   * U.S.-based customization facility offers rapid assembly/testing/integration with local support—flexible client-focused solutions with full package testing.
   * Energy-efficient oil-free scroll compressors require minimal maintenance vs. oil-flooded types; highly competitive total cost of ownership.

   * Top global spray gun manufacturer—dominant market share across Asia; known for reliability/innovation especially in automotive/industrial finishing.

   * [Sources](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anest_Iwata), ([2](https://www.airbestpractices.com/technology/air-compressors/oil-free-technology-anest-iwata)), ([3](https://www.anestiwataamericas.com))
