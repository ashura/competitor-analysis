## Key Features

**ACERETECH:**
- Complete plastic recycling solutions: shredding, crushing, washing, pelletizing.
- Modular and customizable designs for various waste streams.
- High automation and energy efficiency.
- Focus on resource recycling and decarbonization.
- Advanced R&D with over 25 years of team experience.
- Turnkey project support including plant layout and integration.
- International certifications and global after-sales network.

**[Source](https://www.aceretech.com/company-profile)**

**POLYSTAR:**
- Wide range of plastic recycling machines: single/two-stage, shredder-integrated, squeezer dryers.
- Blown film extruder lines for HDPE/LDPE/LLDPE films; multi-layer capabilities (ABA, AB).
- Bag making machines for T-shirt bags, bottom seal bags.
- Simple operation and easy maintenance.
- Customizable solutions for diverse recycling needs.
- Extensive global customer support network.

**[Sources](https://www.polystarco.com/products/recycling-machines/) [Blown Film Machines](https://www.polystarco.com/products/blown-film-machines/)**
