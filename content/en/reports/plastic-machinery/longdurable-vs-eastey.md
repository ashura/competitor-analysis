## Basic Information

**Long Durable Machinery Co., Ltd.**
- Founded in 1995, located in Yunlin County, Taiwan.
- Specializes in fully automatic packaging machines: L-type sealers, sleeve-type sealers, shrink tunnels, carton inserters/erectors, and more.
- Global exporter (Japan, Korea, Australia, England, America, Middle East, Southeast Asia).
- Known for innovation and high standards in packaging machinery.
- [Source](https://www.longdurable.com/msg/company-profile.html)

**Eastey**
- Headquartered in Minnesota, United States.
- Leading manufacturer of heavy-duty packaging machinery: case tapers, L-bar sealers, sleeve wrappers, shrink tunnels, bundling equipment.
- Serves food & beverage, electronics, medical/pharmaceutical industries.
- Part of Engage Technologies; decades of experience; focus on efficiency and durability.
- [Source](https://eastey.com/about/)

## Key Features

**Long Durable Machinery Co., Ltd.**
- Custom automatic packaging solutions.
- Broad product line: L-sealers, sleeve sealers, shrink tunnels, carton bag inserters/sealers, plastic packing machines, palletizers.
- International sales/support network.
- Excellent pre-sale & after-sale service.
- Strong capability for plant-wide automation and tailored solutions.
  - [Source](https://www.longdurable.com/)

**Eastey**
- Heavy-duty steel construction for durability.
- Handles PVC/polyolefin/polyethylene films.
- Manual & automatic equipment options; variable air flow/velocity control in tunnels.
- Electronic tension control; pneumatic cylinders for consistent sealing.
- Minimal training required; robust safety features; cost-effective industrial machines.
  - [Source](https://eastey.com/about/about-us/)
