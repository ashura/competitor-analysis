## Basic Information

**Tai Dragon**
- Founded: 1977
- Location: Taichung, Taiwan
- Focus: Design and manufacture of high-speed horizontal wrapping and packaging machines.
- Product Range: Over 30 models, strong in food packaging but expanding to other industries.
- Export-oriented, known for innovative design and reliability.
  - [Source](https://www.taidragon.com/)
  - [Source](https://www.cens.com/cens/html/en/supplier/supplier_home_3415.html)

**Hopak**
- Founded: 1992
- Location: Taichung, Taiwan
- Focus: High-speed horizontal flow wrappers (HFFS) and automated packaging lines.
- Serves a broad array of industries including food, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, hardware, publishing, and household products.
- Global presence with exports to over 100 countries.
  - [Source](https://www.hopak.com/en/page/company-profile.html)
  - [Source](https://www.pack.org.tw/web/vendor/vendor_in.jsp?subdm_no=DM1576548452004&sp_no=SP1574670150256&lang=en)

## Marketing Focus

**Tai Dragon**
- Emphasizes high-quality machinery (>30 models), innovation/reliability/efficiency.
- Serves both food and non-food sectors with tailored solutions.
- Active in exhibitions and internet marketing.
- Target audience: Medium/large bakeries, food processors, manufacturers needing auto/horizontal flow packaging/custom systems.
  - [Source](https://www.taidragon.com/)

**Hopak**
- Focused on high-speed horizontal flow wrappers and complete packaging lines for a global market.
- Highlights innovation, quality certifications (CE & ISO), customized solutions for diverse customer needs.
- Strong after-sales network and international reach (>100 countries).
- Target audience: Manufacturers in food/pharma/hardware/publishing/household sectors seeking scalable automated systems.
  - [Source](https://www.hopak.com/en/page/company-profile.html)

## Technical Strength

**Tai Dragon**
- Independent R&D department for continuous innovation in over 30 machine types.
- Customizable solutions for diverse applications (food/non-food).
- Integration of robotic arms to enhance automation and reduce labor costs.
- Advanced software systems for stability/high speed/easy maintenance/expandability.
  - [Source](https://www.taidragon.com/en/abouts/about-us)

**Hopak**
- Strong R&D with focus on high-speed HFFS and automation lines.
- In-house development of smart controls and Industry 4.0 integration.
- Certified to international standards (CE & ISO).
- Expertise includes servo control/HMI/flexible automation for various sectors including medical supplies.
  - [Source](https://www.hopak.com/en/page/Company-Profile/company-profile.html)
