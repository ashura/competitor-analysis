## Basic Information

- **ACERETECH Waste Recycle Lines Manufacturer**
  - Founded in 2015, based in Zhangjiagang City, Jiangsu Province, China.
  - Specializes in plastic recycling equipment: shredding, washing, pelletizing, and re-granulating systems.
  - Operates a 16,000 sqm facility with global reach and a focus on innovation and sustainability.
  - [Source](https://www.aceretech.com/company-profile), [Source](https://us.metoree.com/companies/211057/)

- **BEIER MACHINERY**
  - Founded in 1998, also headquartered in Zhangjiagang, Jiangsu Province, China.
  - Four main business sections: plastic recycling/washing lines, compounding/conveying systems, blow molding machines, and European-standard extrusion lines.
  - Over 350 employees and a facility covering more than 160,000 sqm; exports to over 150 countries.
  - Focuses on Industry 4.0, intelligence manufacturing, and global R&D.
  - [Source](https://www.beierrecycling.com/about-us/)
