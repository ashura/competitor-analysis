## Basic Information

**Yi Tzung Precision Machinery Corp**
- Founded in 1946 (as Kim Ho Sheng Iron Factory), renamed Yi Tzung in 1979.
- Headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan.
- Specializes in rubber and plastic machinery for mixing, kneading, calendaring, coating, sheeting, and cooling.
- Serves industries including automotive tires, shoe soles, silicon rubber products, medical and industrial goods.
- Owns a captive foundry (EUCAST) producing about 85% of its components in-house for strict quality control.
- [Source](https://www.yitzung.com.tw/en/about/Company-Profile)

**HF GROUP**
- Leading manufacturer and solution provider in rubber and plastics processing.
- Headquartered in Germany with 15 global sites and ~2,000 employees.
- Focuses on high-performance mixing, curing, extrusion, and automation equipment for tire/rubber/plastics industries.
- Notable for innovations like BANBURY® mixers and advanced digital solutions (HF Xplore®, MyMixingRoom®).
- Strong sustainability emphasis; part of the Possehl Group.
- [Source](https://www.hf-mixinggroup.com/en/company)

## Key Features

- Comprehensive range of machinery for mixing, kneading, calendaring, coating, sheeting, cooling of rubber/EVA.

## Marketing Focus

**Yi Tzung Precision Machinery Corp**
- Focus on supplying advanced mechanical processing equipment to automotive tire manufacturers, footwear producers, electronic component makers (quartz screens), medical/daily-use rubber goods industries.
- Emphasizes strict quality control and in-house manufacturing as key value propositions.
[Source](https://www.yitzung.com.tw)

**HF GROUP**
- Targets B2B customers in the tire/rubber/plastics industries seeking high-performance equipment with sustainability benefits (CO₂ reduction).
- Promotes innovation through digital solutions (online monitoring/customer portal) and comprehensive service/support.
[Source](https://www.hf-mixinggroup.com/en/company)

## Technical Strength

**Yi Tzung Precision Machinery Corp**
- Nearly 80 years’ experience; strong vertical integration with own casting factory (EUCAST).
- Manufactures majority of components internally—enabling consistent quality control from design to assembly/electrical setup.
[Source](https://www.yitzung.com.tw/en/about/Quality-Control-Policy)

**HF GROUP**
- Renowned for technical innovation: Banbury Mixer invention; advanced batch/continuous mixers; digitalization (HF Xplore®, MyMixingRoom®).
- Customizable mixing room systems; focus on process optimization and sustainability with up to 60% CO₂ emissions reduction possible via new tech.
[Source](https://www.hf-mixinggroup.com/en/company/about-us/)
