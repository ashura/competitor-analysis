## Basic Information

**LEO Ultrasonic Co., LTD**
- Founded: 1987, Taiwan
- Location: New Taipei City, Taiwan
- Leadership: Chen Zhi-Mou
- Focus: Manufacturing ultrasonic cleaning machines for diverse industries, with a global export presence in Europe, America, Southeast Asia, etc.
- Highlights: Over 30 years of experience; emphasis on eco-friendly cleaning and automation.
  - [Source](https://www.leo-sonic.com)
  - [Source](https://www.findcompany.com.tw/en/%E5%8A%9B%E9%B4%BB%E5%AF%A6%E6%A5%AD%E6%9C%89%E9%99%90%E5%85%AC%E5%8F%B8)

**Ultrasonic Power Corporation**
- Founded: 1972, USA
- Location: Freeport, Illinois
- Focus: Design, manufacture, and distribution of industrial-grade ultrasonic cleaning equipment for aerospace, automotive, energy, manufacturing, pharmaceutical, music sectors.
- Highlights: All products made in the USA; long-standing reputation for durability and precision.
  - [Source](https://www.upcorp.com/about/)
  - [Source](https://us.metoree.com/companies/124724/)
