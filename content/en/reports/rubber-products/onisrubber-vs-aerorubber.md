## Basic Information

**ONIS Rubber**
- Full Name: JHAO YANG RUBBER (VN) CO., LTD.
- Headquarters: Vietnam (subsidiary of Taiwan-based Jhao Yang Rubber Industrial Co., Ltd.)
- Specializes in custom-molded rubber and silicone products for automotive, electronics, medical, appliances, construction, and food industries.
- Offers injection, compression, and transfer molding technologies.
- Certified to IATF 16949 and ISO 9001 standards.
  - [Source](https://www.onisrubber.com/profile.html)

**Aero Rubber**
- Full Name: Aero Rubber Company®, Inc.
- Headquarters: Tinley Park, Illinois, USA
- Family-run manufacturer of custom rubber components (tubing, sheeting, seals, gaskets) and industrial rubber bands since 1973.
- ISO 9001:2015 certified; provides fabrication services like die cutting and vulcanized splicing.
- Serves a wide range of B2B clients in industries from aerospace to consumer goods.
  - [Source](https://aerorubber.com/about-us/)
