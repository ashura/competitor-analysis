## Basic Information

- **LIAN YU OIL SEAL**
  - Founded in 1978 (some sources cite 1979).
  - Taiwanese manufacturer specializing in oil seals for automotive, shipbuilding, petrochemical, hydraulics, pneumatics, and food processing industries.
  - Offers a wide range of oil seals and rubber parts with a strong focus on quality and customization.
  - Headquarters: Taiwan.
  - Sources:
    [Company website](https://www.lianyuseal.com/company.htm)
    [CENS.com](https://www.cens.com/cens/html/en/supplier/supplier_home_5044.html)
    [Market-Prospects](https://www.market-prospects.com/articles/lian-yu)

- **TAI TSUANG OIL SEAL**
  - Established in 1961.
  - Based in Changhua County, Taiwan; ISO 9001 certified.
  - Originated from a technical joint venture with a Japanese firm; now a global supplier with brand registration in over 60 countries.
  - Manufactures oil seals and rubber products for automotive, motorcycle, marine, industrial/agricultural machinery, and fitness equipment sectors.
  - Facility size: Over 12,000 m²; Employees: ~51–100; Revenue: $50M–$100M.
  - Sources:
    [Company history](https://www.tto.com.tw/en/history)
    [Zoominfo](https://www.zoominfo.com/c/tai-tsuang-oil-seal-industry-co-ltd/441715718)
